# 并列与从属 (Coordination & Subordination)

## 直觉入口

> [!question] 观察这些句子——加粗的连接词把两部分连在一起，但"地位"是否相等？
>
> **A.** She studied hard, **and** she passed the exam.
>
> **B.** She passed the exam **because** she studied hard.
>
> **C.** Not only did she pass, **but** she also topped the class.
>
> **D.** **Although** the evidence was compelling, the jury remained unconvinced.
>
> **E.** The CEO resigned, the stock plummeted, **and** investors panicked.

试着回答：
1. A 和 B 表达的事实相同——为什么感觉 B 更有"重点"？重点在哪部分？
2. C 中 not only...but also 连接的两部分地位相等吗？
3. D 句中，哪部分是"背景"，哪部分是"主要信息"？

> [!note]- 我的假设
> *(并列 = 平等关系？从属 = 主从关系？作者选择哪种结构是否在暗示"哪个更重要"？)*

---

## 中文桥梁

**中文怎么做：**

中文大量使用"意合"——不用连接词，靠语义推断关系：

| 英语（必须标记关系） | 中文（可以省略连词） |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| She passed **because** she studied hard. | 她努力学了，考过了。（因果关系靠语序暗示） |
| **Although** it rained, we went out. | 下雨了，我们还是出去了。（让步关系靠"还是"暗示） |
| He is smart **but** lazy. | 他聪明，就是懒。 |

**关键差异：**
- 中文：连词经常省略，靠语序和语境判断逻辑关系。"意合"语言。
- 英语：逻辑关系**必须用连接词显式标记**——and/but/because/although/however。"形合"语言。

**为什么这对考研重要：**

考研完形填空大量考"选哪个连接词"——本质就是考你能否识别句间逻辑关系。考研阅读中，作者用并列/从属结构暗示信息的"主次关系"——从属从句中的信息是"让步/背景/已知"，主句才是"核心论点"。

> 详见 [[意合与形合]]

## 规则揭示

> [!tip]- 展开核心规则
>
> ### 一、并列 vs 从属的核心区别
>
> | | 并列 (Coordination) | 从属 (Subordination) |
> |--|-------------------|---------------------|
> | 地位 | 两部分平等 | 一主一从 |
> | 连接词 | and, but, or, so, for, yet, nor | because, although, if, when, while, whereas... |
> | 信息权重 | 两部分同等重要 | 主句 > 从句 |
> | 语法独立性 | 两部分各自独立成句 | 从句不能独立成句 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 二、并列连词 (Coordinators)
>
> **七个纯并列连词：** and, but, or, nor, for, yet, so (FANBOYS)
>
> | 连词 | 逻辑关系 | 例句 |
> |------|---------|------|
> | **and** | 添加/顺承 | He sat down **and** opened the book. |
> | **but** | 转折/对比 | She's talented **but** undisciplined. |
> | **or** | 选择/否则 | Hurry up, **or** you'll be late. |
> | **yet** | 转折（更正式） | Simple **yet** effective. |
> | **so** | 结果 | It rained, **so** we stayed in. |
> | **for** | 原因（较文学） | He must be tired, **for** he slept all day. |
> | **nor** | 否定添加 | She didn't call, **nor** did she write. (倒装！) |
>
> ---
>
> ### 三、从属连词 (Subordinators) — 按逻辑关系分类
>
> | 逻辑关系 | 连词 | 信息功能 |
> |---------|------|---------|
> | **原因** | because, since, as, now that, given that | 从句 = 已知原因；主句 = 重点结果 |
> | **让步** | although, though, even though, while, whereas | 从句 = 承认的事实；主句 = 与之相反的主张 |
> | **条件** | if, unless, provided that, as long as, in case | 从句 = 假设条件；主句 = 对应结果 |
> | **时间** | when, while, before, after, until, since, once | 从句 = 时间背景；主句 = 主要事件 |
> | **目的** | so that, in order that, lest | 从句 = 目的；主句 = 为此采取的行动 |
> | **结果** | so...that, such...that | 主句 = 程度；从句 = 结果 |
>
> **考研关键洞察：** 从属从句中的信息 = 作者认为"你已经知道或不争议"的部分。主句 = 作者真正想说的新信息。
>
> ---
>
> ### 四、关联连接词 (Correlative Conjunctions)
>
> | 结构 | 类型 | 要求 | 例句 |
> |------|------|------|------|
> | **both...and** | 并列（肯定） | 平行结构 | **Both** the CEO **and** the board resigned. |
> | **not only...but (also)** | 并列（递进） | 平行 + not only 可倒装 | **Not only** did he pass, **but** he topped the class. |
> | **either...or** | 并列（选择） | 平行结构 | **Either** you agree **or** you leave. |
> | **neither...nor** | 并列（否定） | 平行 + 倒装 | **Neither** the teacher **nor** the students were aware. |
> | **whether...or** | 从属（让步） | | **Whether** you agree **or** not, it's happening. |
>
> ---
>
> ### 五、平行结构 (Parallelism) — 考研写作/完形核心
>
> 并列连接的成分**语法形式必须一致**：
>
> | ✅ 正确（平行） | ❌ 错误（不平行） |
> |----------------|-----------------|
> | She likes **reading** and **writing**. | *She likes **reading** and **to write**. |
> | The plan is **simple**, **effective**, and **affordable**. | *The plan is **simple**, **effective**, and **costs little**. |
> | He decided **to resign** and **to start** his own company. | *He decided **to resign** and **starting** his own company. |
>
> **判断方法：** 找到并列连词（and/or/but），检查两边的语法形式是否镜像匹配。
>
> 考研完形中平行结构的破坏 = 错误选项。写作中使用平行结构 = 句式分。
>
> ---
>
> ### 六、连接副词 (Conjunctive Adverbs) — 不是连词！
>
> | 副词 | 逻辑关系 | 与连词的区别 |
> |------|---------|-------------|
> | **however** | 转折 | ≠ but（however 不能直接连接两个句子） |
> | **therefore** | 结果 | ≠ so |
> | **moreover / furthermore** | 递进 | ≠ and |
> | **nevertheless** | 让步转折 | ≠ although |
> | **consequently** | 因果结果 | ≠ because |
>
> **语法区别：**
> - 连词：He was tired, **but** he continued. ✓（逗号+连词连接两个独立句）
> - 连接副词：He was tired**; however,** he continued. ✓（分号/句号隔开）
> - ❌ *He was tired, **however** he continued.（逗号拼接错误 = comma splice）
>
> ---
>
> ### 七、信息权重策略（考研阅读核心技能）
>
> 作者选择并列还是从属，暗示了信息的"主次"：
>
> | 表达 | 重点在哪 |
> |------|---------|
> | **Although** X, Y. | Y 是重点（X 是让步的背景） |
> | X, **but** Y. | Y 是重点（转折后是作者真正立场） |
> | X **because** Y. | X 是重点（Y 只是解释） |
> | **Because** Y, X. | X 是重点（Y 作为前提铺垫） |
> | X. **However**, Y. | Y 是重点 |
>
> **考研阅读技巧：** 找到作者态度/论点时，优先看主句和转折后的部分。

## 实例分析

### 例句 1（考研阅读 — 让步从属暗示作者真正立场）

> **Although** numerous studies have linked social media use to declining mental health **and** some researchers have even called for outright bans, the evidence remains far from conclusive, **and** the causal mechanisms are poorly understood.
> — *改编自考研阅读*

**逻辑分析：**
```
[Although A and B], C, and D.
  从属从句（让步）：A = 很多研究关联了社交媒体和心理健康下降
                    B = 一些研究者呼吁禁止（and 并列两个让步事实）
  主句（作者真正立场）：C = 证据远非定论
                        D = 因果机制理解不足（and 并列两个主句论点）

→ 作者立场 = 质疑/谨慎，不支持禁令
```

---

### 例句 2（考研完形 — 连接词选择）

> The policy was well-intentioned; ___, its implementation was marred by bureaucratic inefficiency. ___ the designers had anticipated most technical challenges, they failed to account for the human factor — ___ resistance from employees who feared job losses.
> — *改编自考研完形*

**填空分析：**
```
第一空：前面"出发点好"，后面"执行出问题" → 转折 → however/nevertheless
第二空：前面"预见了技术挑战"，后面"没考虑人的因素" → 让步 → Although/While
第三空：解释"人的因素"具体是什么 → namely/specifically
```

---

### 例句 3（学术风格 — 复杂并列 + 平行结构）

> The framework not only integrates findings from neuroscience, psychology, and education **but also** provides practitioners with concrete tools for **assessing** learning outcomes, **identifying** at-risk students, and **designing** targeted interventions.
> — *改编自学术论文*

**平行结构分析：**
```
not only [integrates findings from A, B, and C]  ← 动词 + 宾语
but also [provides practitioners with tools for...]  ← 动词 + 宾语（平行！）

tools for [assessing X], [identifying Y], and [designing Z]
           V-ing + O      V-ing + O          V-ing + O （三重平行！）
```

## 自检

> [!example] 分析以下段落的并列/从属关系，画出信息权重层级，判断作者的核心论点在哪个分句：
> While it is true that automation has displaced certain categories of jobs and that workers in routine-task-intensive occupations have suffered disproportionately, the net effect on employment remains ambiguous, for new technologies have simultaneously created roles that did not previously exist, and historical evidence suggests that economies eventually adapt — though not without significant short-term disruption.

> [!success]- 参考答案
>
> **逻辑层级图：**
> ```
> [While 从属让步]
>   ├── that automation has displaced jobs        ← 已知事实1（让步）
>   └── that workers have suffered               ← 已知事实2（让步）
>
> [主句] the net effect remains ambiguous         ← ★ 作者核心论点
>
> [for 并列原因]
>   ├── new technologies have created roles      ← 支持论据1
>   └── [and 并列] historical evidence suggests economies adapt  ← 支持论据2
>       └── [though 从属让步] not without short-term disruption  ← 承认的限制
> ```
>
> **作者核心立场：** "净效应不确定"（the net effect remains ambiguous）——既不完全同意"自动化消灭就业"，也不天真乐观。
>
> **信息权重：**
> - 最高权重 = 主句（net effect remains ambiguous）
> - 中等权重 = for 后面的两个支持论据
> - 最低权重 = While 让步从句（已知但被作者"降格"的事实）+ though 最后的限定
