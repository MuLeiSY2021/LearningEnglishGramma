# 衔接与连贯 (Cohesion & Coherence)

## 直觉入口

> [!question] 这些句子分别都能看懂，但它们构成一个"段落"吗？
> 
> **版本 A（无衔接）：**
> > The economy grew 3% last year. Many workers lost their jobs. The government announced new policies. Consumer spending declined.
> 
> **版本 B（有衔接）：**
> > The economy grew 3% last year. **However**, many workers **still** lost **their** jobs. **In response**, the government announced new policies. **Meanwhile**, consumer spending **continued to** decline.
> 
> 两个版本包含相同的事实。但版本 B 读起来像一个连贯的"故事"，版本 A 像四个不相干的新闻标题。为什么？

> [!note]- 我的假设
> *(加了什么东西？这些东西的功能是什么？)*

---

## 中文桥梁

**回到意合与形合：** （详见 [[意合与形合]]）

中文读者看版本 A 可能不觉得太突兀——因为中文天然允许读者自己推断逻辑关系（意合）。"经济增长，工人失业"——你的大脑自动补了"但是"的转折关系。

但英文是**形合**语言——逻辑关系必须**写出来**。如果不写，英文读者会觉得：
- 这些句子为什么放在一起？
- 它们之间是什么关系？
- 作者在说什么？

**这就是衔接 (cohesion) 的意义：** 用显性的语言标记把句子"粘合"成段落。

**衔接 vs 连贯：**
- **衔接 (Cohesion)** = 句子之间的**语言纽带**（代词、连接词、重复...）— 表面可见
- **连贯 (Coherence)** = 段落的**逻辑通顺感** — 深层意义

衔接是工具，连贯是效果。有衔接不一定连贯（乱用 however/therefore 反而混乱），但英文写作中没有衔接几乎不可能连贯。

## 规则揭示

> [!tip]- 五大衔接手段 (Halliday & Hasan)
> 
> ### 1. 指代 (Reference)
> 
> 用代词或指示词**指回**前文提到的事物，避免重复。
> 
> > The researchers published **their** findings. **They** concluded that...
> > 
> > A new policy was announced. **This** immediately sparked debate.
> > 
> > Two options were proposed: the **former** focused on cost, the **latter** on quality.
> 
> **常见指代词：**
> he, she, it, they, this, that, these, those, such, the former, the latter, the same
> 
> **阅读策略：** 看到代词/this/that → 问自己"指的是前面的什么？"
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 2. 替代 (Substitution)
> 
> 用一个"替身词"代替前面出现过的整个短语。
> 
> > "Would you like a large coffee?" — "I'll have a small **one**."
> > (one = coffee)
> > 
> > "Did she pass the exam?" — "I believe **so**."
> > (so = that she passed the exam)
> > 
> > "Will it rain tomorrow?" — "I hope **not**."
> > (not = that it won't rain)
> 
> **常见替代词：** one/ones, do/do so, so, not, the same
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 3. 省略 (Ellipsis)
> 
> 直接删除前面出现过的重复内容——读者能从上下文补全。
> 
> > "Would you like tea or coffee?" — "Coffee [~~would be fine~~]."
> > 
> > He can play the piano and she [~~can play~~] the violin.
> > 
> > Some students passed; others [~~students~~] didn't [~~pass~~].
> 
> **中文对照：** 中文本来就大量省略（你以为中文不省略？"你吃了吗？""吃了。"），所以这对你来说是最直觉的衔接手段。英文也能省略，但条件更严格——必须在语法上能恢复原句。
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 4. 连接 (Conjunction / Discourse Connectors)
> 
> 用连接词标明前后句子的**逻辑关系**。这是中国学习者最需要掌握的——因为中文几乎不用这些词。
> 
> | 关系类型 | 连接词 | 功能 |
> |----------|--------|------|
> | **添加** (Additive) | moreover, furthermore, in addition, also, besides, what is more | 补充同方向的信息 |
> | **转折** (Adversative) | however, nevertheless, on the other hand, yet, nonetheless, still, in contrast | 转向相反方向 |
> | **因果** (Causal) | therefore, consequently, as a result, hence, thus, for this reason, accordingly | 表示原因或结果 |
> | **时间** (Temporal) | subsequently, meanwhile, previously, eventually, thereafter, in the meantime, finally | 标明时间先后 |
> | **让步** (Concessive) | admittedly, of course, granted, it is true that... nevertheless | 先承认再转折 |
> | **总结** (Summarizing) | in conclusion, to sum up, overall, in short, ultimately | 概括前文 |
> | **举例** (Exemplifying) | for example, for instance, specifically, in particular, namely | 提供具体例证 |
> 
> **注意位置和标点：**
> > **However,** the results were inconclusive. （句首 + 逗号）
> > The results**,** however**,** were inconclusive. （句中插入 + 两个逗号）
> > The results were inconclusive**,** however. （句尾 + 逗号）
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 5. 词汇衔接 (Lexical Cohesion)
> 
> 不用代词，而是通过**词汇本身的关系**来建立连接。
> 
> **重复 (Repetition)：**
> > The **government** announced new measures. The **government's** critics responded sharply.
> 
> **同义词/近义词 (Synonymy)：**
> > The **country** faced a crisis. The **nation** had never experienced anything like it.
> 
> **上下义词 (Hyponymy)：**
> > Several **animals** escaped from the zoo. A **lion** was spotted near the highway.
> > (lion 是 animal 的下义词)
> 
> **搭配 (Collocation)：**
> > The **trial** lasted three weeks. The **jury** deliberated for two days before reaching a **verdict**.
> > (trial - jury - verdict 是同一语义场的搭配词)

## 实例分析

### 一段真实文本的衔接标注

> *Climate change poses an unprecedented threat to coastal cities. **These urban areas** [指代+词汇衔接] house more than a billion people worldwide. **Rising sea levels** [搭配: climate change → sea levels], **in particular** [举例连接], endanger low-lying regions in Southeast Asia. **The problem** [上义词: threat → problem], **however** [转折连接], is not merely environmental. **It** [指代] also carries profound economic implications, as property values decline and insurance costs skyrocket. **Consequently** [因果连接], governments must act now — not in decades, but in years.*

**衔接手段统计：**
- 指代：These, It（指回前面提到的事物）
- 连接词：in particular, however, consequently（标注逻辑关系）
- 词汇衔接：climate change → rising sea levels（搭配）；threat → problem（同义/上义）；coastal cities → urban areas → low-lying regions（同义链）
- 省略：无明显省略

**如果去掉所有衔接：**
> ~~Climate change threatens coastal cities. Urban areas house a billion people. Sea levels endanger Southeast Asia. Things are not just environmental. Economic implications exist. Governments must act now.~~

对比一下——事实相同，但读起来像机器人在列举数据点，不是在"论证"。

## 自检

> [!example] 识别以下段落中所有衔接手段，并标注类型：
> 
> "The company reported record profits last quarter. **This success**, **however**, came at a cost. Employee satisfaction surveys revealed widespread burnout, **particularly** among junior staff. **Many** had been working 70-hour weeks. **As a result**, turnover rates doubled, and **the firm** struggled to retain **its** top talent."

> [!success]- 参考答案
> 
> | 衔接标记 | 类型 | 指向 |
> |----------|------|------|
> | **This success** | 指代 + 词汇（概括）| = "record profits" 这件事 |
> | **however** | 转折连接 | 利润高→但有代价 |
> | **particularly** | 举例/聚焦连接 | 具体到哪些人 |
> | **Many** | 指代（省略了 of them）| = junior staff |
> | **As a result** | 因果连接 | 加班→离职率翻倍 |
> | **the firm** | 词汇衔接（同义）| = the company |
> | **its** | 指代 | = the firm's |
> 
> 衔接链：company → firm → its（词汇+指代链保持话题一致）
> 逻辑链：利润高 → (however) 有代价 → (particularly) 具体到基层 → (as a result) 结果是人才流失
