# 谜题 1.4: 通关测试

## 说明

这是第一层的综合检测。六个句子覆盖全部五种句型，来源混杂，难度不均。不看提示完成 = 你已经准备好进入第二层。

---

## 原文

> **A.** "The patient's condition deteriorated rapidly overnight."
> — 医学报告

> **B.** "I'll tell you a secret."
> — 日常对话

> **C.** "The unprecedented scale of the disaster rendered traditional rescue methods completely inadequate."
> — *BBC News*

> **D.** "The sea was angry that day, my friends."
> — *Seinfeld, George Costanza*

> **E.** "A specter is haunting Europe — the specter of communism."
> — *Karl Marx, The Communist Manifesto*

> **F.** "Despite the mounting evidence and repeated warnings from health officials, the government's response to the crisis remained slow and ineffective."
> — *The Guardian*

---

## 任务

> [!question] 你的任务：
> 1. 为每句找出骨架（S + V + 后续成分）
> 2. 判断句型（SV / SVO / SVC / SVOO / SVOC）
> 3. 指出哪些部分是可以剥离的修饰层

---

## 提示

> [!hint]- 提示 1（轻）
> 句 A 的动词后面没有宾语——"rapidly" 和 "overnight" 都是副词/状语。

> [!hint]- 提示 2（中）
> 句 C 的关键：rendered X Y = 使 X 变成 Y 的状态。这是哪种句型？

> [!hint]- 提示 3（重）
> - A: SV（deteriorated 是不及物动词）
> - B: SVOO（tell + 你 + 一个秘密）
> - C: SVOC（rendered + methods + inadequate）
> - D: SVC（sea = angry）
> - E: SVO（specter is haunting Europe）
> - F: SVC（response = slow and ineffective）

## 解答

> [!success]- 完整解析
> 
> **A.** "The patient's condition deteriorated rapidly overnight."
> ```
> [The patient's condition] [deteriorated]
>            S                    V
> ```
> 句型：**SV** — deteriorated（恶化）是不及物动词
> "rapidly" 和 "overnight" 都是状语，修饰动词
> 
> ---
> 
> **B.** "I'll tell you a secret."
> ```
> [I] ['ll tell] [you] [a secret]
>  S      V       IO      DO
> ```
> 句型：**SVOO** — tell somebody something
> 
> ---
> 
> **C.** "The unprecedented scale of the disaster rendered traditional rescue methods completely inadequate."
> ```
> [The scale] [rendered] [traditional rescue methods] [inadequate]
>      S          V                O                      OC
> ```
> 句型：**SVOC** — methods = inadequate（宾语和宾补有"="关系）
> 
> 修饰层剥离：
> - "unprecedented" → 修饰 scale
> - "of the disaster" → 修饰 scale
> - "traditional rescue" → 修饰 methods
> - "completely" → 修饰 inadequate
> 
> ---
> 
> **D.** "The sea was angry that day, my friends."
> ```
> [The sea] [was] [angry]
>     S      V      C
> ```
> 句型：**SVC** — sea = angry
> "that day" 是时间状语，"my friends" 是称呼语（插入语）
> 
> ---
> 
> **E.** "A specter is haunting Europe — the specter of communism."
> ```
> [A specter] [is haunting] [Europe]
>      S           V           O
> ```
> 句型：**SVO** — 进行时态（is haunting）
> 破折号后面是对 "a specter" 的同位语补充说明
> 
> ---
> 
> **F.** "Despite the mounting evidence and repeated warnings from health officials, the government's response to the crisis remained slow and ineffective."
> ```
> [the government's response] [remained] [slow and ineffective]
>              S                   V                C
> ```
> 句型：**SVC** — response = slow and ineffective
> 
> 修饰层剥离：
> - "Despite the mounting evidence and repeated warnings from health officials" → 介词短语做让步状语
> - "to the crisis" → 修饰 response
> - remained 是系动词（= 保持处于...状态）
> 
> **涉及语法：**
> - [[五大基本句型]]
> - 系动词的判定（was, remained）
> - SVOC 的 "宾语=宾补" 测试
> - 状语与核心成分的分离
