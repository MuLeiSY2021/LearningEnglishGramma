# 谜题 1.2: 新闻长句的骨架

## 原文

这些句子来自英语新闻，长度和修饰远超日常对话。你的目标只有一个：**找到骨架**。

> **A.** "The decision by the Federal Reserve to raise interest rates for the tenth consecutive time caught many investors, who had been betting on a pause, completely off guard."
> — *NYT*

> **B.** "What began as a small protest in a remote province has, in the space of just three weeks, grown into a nationwide movement that threatens to topple the government."
> — *The Economist*

> **C.** "Scientists at the University of Cambridge have for the first time successfully demonstrated a method of converting atmospheric carbon dioxide into solid carbon at room temperature."
> — *BBC*

---

## 任务

> [!question] 你的任务：
> 1. 找到主语和主动词（忽略所有从句、介词短语、插入语）
> 2. 判断句型
> 3. 注意：有些主语本身就很长——找到主语的"核心词"

---

## 提示

> [!hint]- 提示 1（轻）
> 句 A 的主语不是 "The Federal Reserve"。"by the Federal Reserve" 是介词短语，修饰前面的名词。

> [!hint]- 提示 2（中）
> 句 B 的主语是一个从句（What began as...）。主动词在逗号插入语之后。

> [!hint]- 提示 3（重）
> - A: 主语 = The decision, 动词 = caught
> - B: 主语 = What began as a small protest, 动词 = has grown
> - C: 主语 = Scientists, 动词 = have demonstrated

## 解答

> [!success]- 完整解析
> 
> **A.**
> ```
> [The decision] [caught] [many investors] [off guard]
>       S           V          O              OC
> ```
> 句型：**SVOC** — caught sb off guard（使某人措手不及）
> 
> 修饰层剥离：
> - "by the Federal Reserve" → 修饰 decision
> - "to raise interest rates for the tenth consecutive time" → 修饰 decision
> - "who had been betting on a pause" → 关系从句，修饰 investors
> - "completely" → 修饰 off guard
> 
> ---
> 
> **B.**
> ```
> [What began as a small protest in a remote province] [has grown] [into a nationwide movement]
>                         S                                V              介词补语
> ```
> 句型：**SV** + 介词补语（grow into = 发展成）
> 
> 修饰层剥离：
> - "in the space of just three weeks" → 时间状语（插入在 has 和 grown 之间！）
> - "that threatens to topple the government" → 关系从句，修饰 movement
> 
> 注意：主语是整个 What 从句（名词从句做主语，第三层会详解）
> 
> ---
> 
> **C.**
> ```
> [Scientists] [have demonstrated] [a method]
>      S              V                O
> ```
> 句型：**SVO**
> 
> 修饰层剥离：
> - "at the University of Cambridge" → 修饰 Scientists
> - "for the first time" → 时间状语（插入在 have 和 demonstrated 之间）
> - "successfully" → 修饰 demonstrated
> - "of converting atmospheric carbon dioxide into solid carbon at room temperature" → 修饰 method
> 
> **涉及语法：**
> - [[五大基本句型]]
> - 名词短语的后置修饰（第二层）
> - 状语的插入位置

---

## 举一反三

练习找这类句子的骨架——诀窍是：
1. 跳过 by/of/in/at/for 开头的介词短语
2. 跳过 who/which/that 引导的从句
3. 跳过两个逗号之间的插入语
4. 剩下的就是骨架
