# 谜题 1.1: 游戏台词中的骨架

## 原文

逐一找出每个句子的骨架（主语 + 主动词 + 后续成分）。

> **A.** "Every age, it seems, is tainted by the greed of men."
> — *Dark Souls III*

> **B.** "The Lands Between, governed by Queen Marika the Eternal, are blessed by the presence of the Erdtree."
> — *Elden Ring*

> **C.** "In truth, I outlived you all long ago."
> — *Baldur's Gate 3, Withers*

> **D.** "What is bravery without a dash of recklessness?"
> — *Dark Souls III, Hawkwood*

---

## 任务

> [!question] 你的任务：
> 1. 为每个句子找到主语（S）和主动词（V）
> 2. 判断每句属于哪种句型（SV / SVO / SVC / SVOO / SVOC）
> 3. 指出哪些成分是"修饰/插入"，可以暂时忽略

---

## 提示

> [!hint]- 提示 1（轻）
> 句 B 里有两个逗号之间夹着的部分——那是插入语，先跳过它。

> [!hint]- 提示 2（中）
> 句 A 的 "it seems" 是插入语，去掉不影响结构。去掉它后，注意这是被动句——真正的动作执行者藏在 "by..." 后面，但语法主语仍然是 every age。

> [!hint]- 提示 3（重）
> - A: 被动句，主语是 "every age"，被动动词 "is tainted"
> - B: 被动句，主语是 "The Lands Between"，被动动词 "are blessed"
> - C: 主动句，SVO
> - D: SVC，"What" 是主语

## 解答

> [!success]- 完整解析
> 
> **A.** "Every age, it seems, is tainted by the greed of men."
> ```
> [Every age] [is tainted] (by the greed of men)
>      S         V(被动)        施动者
> ```
> 句型：SV（被动态）— age 被 taint（玷污）
> 插入语："it seems"（看起来）可以完全删除不影响结构
> 
> ---
> 
> **B.** "The Lands Between, governed by Queen Marika the Eternal, are blessed by the presence of the Erdtree."
> ```
> [The Lands Between] [are blessed] (by the presence of the Erdtree)
>          S              V(被动)           施动者
> ```
> 句型：SV（被动态）
> 插入语："governed by Queen Marika the Eternal" 是过去分词短语，修饰 Lands Between
> 
> ---
> 
> **C.** "In truth, I outlived you all long ago."
> ```
> [I] [outlived] [you all]
>  S      V         O
> ```
> 句型：SVO
> "In truth" 是状语（可删除），"long ago" 是时间状语
> 
> ---
> 
> **D.** "What is bravery without a dash of recklessness?"
> ```
> [bravery] [is] [what] (without a dash of recklessness)
>     S      V     C
> ```
> 句型：SVC — bravery = what (nothing)
> 这是倒装的修辞问句。正常语序：Bravery is what without recklessness?（= nothing）
> "without a dash of recklessness" 是条件状语
> 
> **涉及语法：**
> - [[五大基本句型]]
> - 被动语态（第二层会详解）
> - 插入语
