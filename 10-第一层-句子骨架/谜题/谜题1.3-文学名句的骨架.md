# 谜题 1.3: 文学名句的骨架

## 原文

文学语言往往在简单骨架上堆叠华丽的修饰。找到那根骨头。

> **A.** "It was a bright cold day in April, and the clocks were striking thirteen."
> — *George Orwell, 1984*

> **B.** "In my younger and more vulnerable years my father gave me some advice that I've been turning over in my mind ever since."
> — *F. Scott Fitzgerald, The Great Gatsby*

> **C.** "The mystery of life isn't a problem to solve, but a reality to experience."
> — *Frank Herbert, Dune*

> **D.** "All the stories and descriptions of that period without exception speak of nothing but the self-sacrifice, patriotic devotion, despair, grief, and the heroism of the Russians."
> — *Leo Tolstoy, War and Peace*

---

## 任务

> [!question] 你的任务：
> 1. 找骨架（S + V + O/C）
> 2. 句 A 有两个骨架——用什么连接？
> 3. 句 D 的主语异常长——它的核心词是什么？

---

## 提示

> [!hint]- 提示 1（轻）
> 句 B 开头的 "In my younger and more vulnerable years" 不是主语——它是状语。真正的主语在它后面。

> [!hint]- 提示 2（中）
> 句 C 用了 "isn't... but..." 对比结构。骨架是 SVC，只是补语有两个对称部分。

> [!hint]- 提示 3（重）
> - A: It was a day + clocks were striking thirteen（两个 SVC/SV，用 and 连接）
> - B: father gave me some advice（SVOO）
> - C: mystery isn't a problem but a reality（SVC）
> - D: stories and descriptions speak of nothing but...（SV + 介词补语）

## 解答

> [!success]- 完整解析
> 
> **A.** 两个并列句
> ```
> [It] [was] [a bright cold day]  AND  [the clocks] [were striking] [thirteen]
>  S    V         C                        S             V              O
> ```
> 句型：SVC + SVO，用 and 并列
> 
> 注意：strike 用于报时时，thirteen 可看作宾语（SVO：钟敲了十三下）也可看作状语（SV：钟在报十三点）。这里简化为 SVO 处理——重要的是你能找到骨架。
> 
> ---
> 
> **B.**
> ```
> [my father] [gave] [me] [some advice]
>      S        V    IO       DO
> ```
> 句型：**SVOO**（给某人某物）
> 
> 修饰层：
> - "In my younger and more vulnerable years" → 时间状语
> - "that I've been turning over in my mind ever since" → 关系从句修饰 advice
> 
> ---
> 
> **C.**
> ```
> [The mystery of life] [isn't] [a problem to solve, but a reality to experience]
>          S               V                    C
> ```
> 句型：**SVC** — mystery = not a problem, but a reality
> 
> ---
> 
> **D.**
> ```
> [All the stories and descriptions of that period without exception] [speak] (of nothing but...)
>                                   S                                    V        介词补语
> ```
> 句型：**SV** + 介词补语
> 
> 主语核心词："stories and descriptions"（并列名词）
> "of that period" 和 "without exception" 都是修饰主语的介词短语
> 
> **涉及语法：**
> - [[五大基本句型]]
> - 并列句（and 连接两个独立主句）
> - 超长主语的核心词识别

---

## 举一反三

文学句子的特点：骨架常常很简单（SVC、SVO），复杂性来自修饰的层叠。找骨架时，先定位动词，再往两边扩展。
