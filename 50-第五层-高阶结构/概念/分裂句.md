# 分裂句 (Cleft Sentences)

## 直觉入口

> [!question] 这三句话意思相同——为什么要用不同的句式？各自在"强调"什么？
>
> **A.** John broke the window yesterday.
>
> **B.** **It was John** that broke the window yesterday.
>
> **C.** **What John broke** yesterday **was the window**.
>
> **D.** **It was yesterday** that John broke the window.

试着回答：
1. A 是中性叙述。B/C/D 各自把焦点放在哪个成分上？
2. B 和 D 结构相同（It was...that），但强调的对象不同——怎么判断强调的是谁？
3. "It was...that" 和 "It is obvious that..."（形式主语）长得很像——怎么区分？

> [!note]- 我的假设
> *(试着用"去掉 It is/was...that"的方法，看 B 句剩下什么：John broke the window yesterday. 如果还原成完整句子 → 强调句。)*

---

## 中文桥梁

**中文怎么做：**

中文用"是…的"结构来强调：

| 英语分裂句 | 中文强调句 |
|-----------|-----------|
| **It was John** that broke the window. | **是约翰**打破了窗户。 |
| **It was yesterday** that he left. | 他**是昨天**走的。 |
| **What I need** is a vacation. | 我需要的**是**一个假期。 |

**关键差异：**
- 中文"是…的"很自然，日常大量使用。
- 英语分裂句更正式，学术写作和考研文章中频繁出现。
- 难点不在于理解"强调了什么"，而在于**和形式主语/关系从句混淆**。

**为什么英语需要分裂句：**

英语语序相对固定（SVO），不能像中文那样随意调整语序来突出焦点。分裂句是英语在不改变基本语序的情况下"把聚光灯打在某个成分上"的工具。

## 规则揭示

> [!tip]- 展开核心规则
>
> ### 一、It 分裂句 (It-cleft)
>
> **结构：** `It is/was + 被强调成分 + that/who + 其余部分`
>
> 原句 John broke the window yesterday 可以强调不同成分：
>
> | 强调对象 | 分裂句 |
> |---------|--------|
> | 主语 | **It was John** that/who broke the window yesterday. |
> | 宾语 | **It was the window** that John broke yesterday. |
> | 时间状语 | **It was yesterday** that John broke the window. |
> | 原因状语 | **It was because of the storm** that the flight was cancelled. |
>
> **判断方法（去框架法）：** 去掉 "It is/was...that"，剩余部分能否拼成完整句？
> - It was **John** that **broke the window yesterday** → John broke the window yesterday ✓ → 强调句
> - It is **obvious** that **he lied** → *Obvious he lied ✗ → 形式主语（不是强调句）
>
> ---
>
> ### 二、It-cleft vs 形式主语 vs 关系从句（三重辨析）
>
> | 结构 | 例句 | 判断方法 |
> |------|------|---------|
> | **强调句** | It was the CEO **that** resigned. | 去掉框架 → The CEO resigned ✓ |
> | **形式主语** | It is clear **that** he resigned. | 去掉框架 → *Clear he resigned ✗；且 it = that 从句 |
> | **关系从句** | It was a decision **that** changed history. | it ≠ 框架，it = a decision；that 从句修饰 decision |
>
> **快速三问：**
> 1. 去掉 It is/was...that，剩余能成句？→ **强调句**
> 2. It 能被 that 从句替换？（That he resigned is clear）→ **形式主语**
> 3. It 指代具体名词，that 从句修饰该名词？→ **关系从句**
>
> ---
>
> ### 三、Wh-分裂句 / 伪分裂句 (Pseudo-cleft)
>
> **结构：** `What/The thing that + S + V + is/was + 被强调部分`
>
> | 例句 | 强调什么 |
> |------|---------|
> | **What I need** is a long vacation. | 强调 a long vacation |
> | **What surprised me** was his calmness. | 强调 his calmness |
> | **What the government did** was raise taxes. | 强调 raise taxes |
> | **The reason why he left** was that he felt undervalued. | 强调原因 |
>
> **反转伪分裂句：** 也可以把被强调部分放前面：
> > A long vacation is **what I need**.
>
> **与 It-cleft 的区别：**
> - It-cleft 更书面、正式
> - Wh-cleft 更口语化，且 what 从句做主语时强调"对比"（你以为是 X，其实是 Y）
>
> ---
>
> ### 四、强调句中的时态和否定
>
> **时态：** It is/was 的时态跟随原句：
> - 原句过去时 → It **was**...that
> - 原句现在时 → It **is**...that
>
> **否定：** 否定可以放在 It 部分或 that 从句中：
> - **It was not** until midnight **that** she called. = 直到午夜她才打电话。
> - not until 的强调句是考研高频结构！
>
> **疑问：**
> - **Was it John** that broke it? （一般疑问）
> - **Who was it** that broke it? （特殊疑问）
>
> ---
>
> ### 五、考研高频搭配：It is not until...that
>
> 这是考研翻译和阅读中最常见的强调句形式：
>
> | 原句 | 强调句 |
> |------|--------|
> | She didn't realize the truth **until** she read the letter. | **It was not until** she read the letter **that** she realized the truth. |
> | We won't know **until** the results come in. | **It will not be until** the results come in **that** we know. |
>
> **翻译公式：** It is not until A that B = 直到 A 才 B。

## 实例分析

### 例句 1（考研翻译 — It is not until...that）

> **It was not until** the industrial revolution dramatically altered the relationship between humans and nature **that** environmental degradation began to be perceived as a serious threat.
> — *改编自考研翻译*

**拆解：**
```
It was not until [the industrial revolution dramatically altered...]  ← 被强调的时间状语
that [environmental degradation began to be perceived...]            ← 主句剩余部分

还原：Environmental degradation didn't begin to be perceived as a threat
      until the industrial revolution altered the relationship...
```

**中文对照：** 直到工业革命极大地改变了人与自然的关系，环境退化才开始被视为严重威胁。

---

### 例句 2（考研阅读 — 强调句 vs 形式主语混合）

> **It is** precisely this capacity for abstract thought **that** distinguishes humans from other species, though **it is** also true **that** many animals exhibit rudimentary forms of reasoning.
> — *改编自考研阅读*

**辨析：**
```
第一个 It is...that:
  去掉框架 → This capacity for abstract thought distinguishes humans... ✓
  → 强调句！强调 "this capacity for abstract thought"

第二个 It is...that:
  去掉框架 → *Also true many animals exhibit... ✗
  it = that 从句（that many animals exhibit...）
  → 形式主语！It is true that... = That many animals exhibit... is true.
```

---

### 例句 3（学术风格 — Wh-分裂句）

> **What these findings ultimately suggest** is not that genetics determines destiny, but rather that environment and genetics interact in ways **that** we are only beginning to understand.
> — *改编自 Nature*

**拆解：**
```
[What these findings ultimately suggest]  ← Wh-分裂句主语部分
is                                        ← 系动词
not that X, but rather that Y             ← 被强调的内容（表语）
  not that genetics determines destiny ← that 名词从句
  but rather that environment and genetics interact... ← that 名词从句
    in ways [that we are only beginning to understand] ← that 关系从句修饰 ways
```

**中文对照：** 这些发现最终表明的不是基因决定命运，而是环境与基因以我们才刚刚开始理解的方式相互作用。

---

### 例句 4（考研阅读 — 三种 "It...that" 同段辨析）

> **It is** widely acknowledged **that** education reduces inequality. However, **it was** the 2008 financial crisis **that** exposed how fragile this assumption had become. **It** remains a policy **that** few governments dare to cut.
> — *改编自考研阅读*

**逐句辨析：**

| 句子 | It...that 类型 | 判断依据 |
|------|---------------|---------|
| It is widely acknowledged **that** education reduces... | 形式主语 | 去掉框架：*Widely acknowledged education reduces... ✗ |
| It was the 2008 financial crisis **that** exposed... | **强调句** | 去掉框架：The 2008 financial crisis exposed... ✓ |
| It remains a policy **that** few governments dare to cut. | **关系从句** | it = a policy（指代）；that 从句修饰 policy（缺宾语：cut ___） |

## 自检

> [!example] 判断以下段落中每个 "It...that" 结构的类型（强调句/形式主语/关系从句），并解释判断依据：
> It has been argued that social media accelerates polarization. But it was not the technology itself that created division — it was the business model that prioritized engagement over truth. It is this fundamental misalignment that policymakers must address, for it is no longer possible that society can afford to wait.

> [!success]- 参考答案
>
> | # | 结构 | 类型 | 判断 |
> |---|------|------|------|
> | ① | **It** has been argued **that** social media accelerates... | **形式主语** | It = that 从句；去掉框架 *Has been argued social media... ✗ |
> | ② | **it was** not the technology itself **that** created division | **强调句** | 去掉：Not the technology itself created division → The technology itself didn't create division ✓ |
> | ③ | **it was** the business model **that** prioritized engagement... | **强调句** | 去掉：The business model prioritized engagement over truth ✓ |
> | ④ | **It is** this fundamental misalignment **that** policymakers must address | **强调句** | 去掉：Policymakers must address this fundamental misalignment ✓ |
> | ⑤ | **it is** no longer possible **that** society can afford to wait | **形式主语** | It = that 从句；去掉框架 *No longer possible society can afford... ✗ |
>
> **核心技巧总结：** "去框架法"一步到位——去掉 It is/was...that，看剩余部分能否成句。能 = 强调句；不能 = 形式主语或关系从句。
