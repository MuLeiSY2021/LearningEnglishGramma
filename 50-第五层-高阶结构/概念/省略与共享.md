# 省略与共享 (Ellipsis & Gapping)

## 直觉入口

> [!question] 这些句子中缺了什么？试着把"省略的部分"补回去。
>
> **A.** He ordered coffee and she _ tea.
>
> **B.** The project was more difficult than we had expected _.
>
> **C.** Some students passed the exam; others didn't _.
>
> **D.** "Can you help me?" — "I'd like to _, but I'm busy."
>
> **E.** She plays the piano better than he _ the guitar.

试着回答：
1. 每个 _ 处省略了什么词？
2. 为什么省略后仍然能理解意思？
3. E 句省略后还剩什么？这样的省略在中文里可以吗？

> [!note]- 我的假设
> *(英语的省略是"偷懒"还是有规则的？什么时候可以省，什么时候不能？)*

---

## 中文桥梁

**中文怎么做：**

中文也大量省略重复信息，而且比英语更自由：

| 英语（有规则限制） | 中文（几乎任意省略） |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| He likes tea and she _ coffee. | 他喝茶，她喝咖啡。（也省了"喜欢"） |
| Better than expected. | 比预期的好。 |
| "Will you come?" — "I will _." | "你来吗？" — "来。" |

**关键差异：**
- 中文：只要上下文能推断，几乎什么都能省——主语、动词、宾语全可以省。
- 英语：省略有严格的语法规则——必须是"结构平行"中的"重复部分"才能省。不能随便省主语（*Likes tea 不合法），也不能省一半动词。

**为什么中国学生觉得英语省略难：**

不是因为英语省略多（其实中文省略更多），而是英语省略后**留下的残余结构看起来不像完整句子**。比如 "more than expected" 让人疑惑——谁 expected？expected 什么？中国学生不习惯"把省略的部分还原"这个阅读技能。

## 规则揭示

> [!tip]- 展开核心规则
>
> ### 一、省略的类型总览
>
> | 类型 | 结构 | 省略什么 | 例句 |
> |------|------|---------|------|
> | **Gapping（动词省略）** | 并列句中省略重复动词 | V | He likes tea, she _ coffee. |
> | **VP 省略** | 用助动词替代整个动词短语 | VP | "Can you swim?" — "Yes, I **can** _." |
> | **名词省略** | 用 one/ones/that/those 替代 | NP | I want the **red one**, not the blue _. |
> | **比较省略** | than/as 从句中省略重复部分 | 各种 | She runs faster than he _ [runs]. |
> | **不定式省略** | 保留 to，省略后面的动词 | V after to | "Will you stay?" — "I'd like **to** _." |
> | **共享（Right Node Raising）** | 并列的两个动词共享同一个宾语 | 第一个O | She can _ and will _ solve this problem. |
>
> ---
>
> ### 二、Gapping（并列中省略动词）
>
> **规则：** 两个并列分句结构相同时，第二个分句可以省略动词（和部分助动词）。
>
> | 完整 | 省略后 |
> |------|--------|
> | Tom studies physics and Mary **studies** chemistry. | Tom studies physics and Mary _ chemistry. |
> | Some agreed, but others **did not agree**. | Some agreed, but others didn't _. |
> | He could have helped, but he **didn't help**. | He could have, but he didn't _. |
>
> **限制：** 只能在第二个（或后续）并列分句中省略——不能省第一个。
>
> ---
>
> ### 三、比较从句中的省略（考研核心）
>
> 比较从句（than / as 后面）中大量省略重复部分：
>
> | 省略后 | 还原 | 省略了什么 |
> |--------|------|-----------|
> | harder than **expected** | harder than **it was** expected **to be** | 主语 + be + to be |
> | more than **necessary** | more than **it is** necessary **to have** | 主语 + be |
> | as much as **possible** | as much as **it is** possible **to do** | 主语 + be + to do |
> | better than **he** | better than he **does/is** | 动词 |
> | different from **what was initially proposed** | — (完整) | — |
>
> **还原方法：**
> 1. 找到 than/as
> 2. 确定主句中被比较的成分
> 3. 用主句对应结构补全从句
>
> > 详见 [[比较结构]]
>
> ---
>
> ### 四、VP 省略（助动词后省略）
>
> 保留助动词/情态动词，省略后面的整个动词短语：
>
> | 完整 | 省略后 |
> |------|--------|
> | She hasn't finished, but I **have finished**. | She hasn't finished, but I **have**. |
> | "Will you help?" — "I **will help**." | "Will you help?" — "I **will**." |
> | He may not come, but she **might come**. | He may not come, but she **might**. |
>
> **相关结构：**
> - **so do I** = I do the same thing（肯定）
> - **neither/nor did he** = he didn't either（否定）
>
> ---
>
> ### 五、不定式省略（保留 to）
>
> 在 want to, hope to, like to, be going to 等结构中，可以只保留 to：
>
> | 完整 | 省略后 |
> |------|--------|
> | I don't want to **leave**, but I have to **leave**. | I don't want to, but I have to. |
> | "Will you resign?" — "I don't intend to **resign**." | "Will you resign?" — "I don't intend to." |
>
> **例外：** be/have 作为主动词时，保留它们而不只留 to：
> - "Are you a teacher?" — "I used to **be**." （不能说 *I used to.）
>
> ---
>
> ### 六、共享结构 (Right Node Raising)
>
> 两个并列成分共享右边的成分：
>
> ```
> She [can _] and [will _] finish the project on time.
>       ↑           ↑
>       两个情态动词共享宾语 "finish the project on time"
> ```
>
> 更复杂的例子：
> > The policy both [encouraged _] and [provided incentives for _] private investment.
> > 两个动词共享同一个宾语 private investment
>
> **考研阅读中的识别：** 看到 both...and / not only...but also / either...or 并列两个动词时，宾语可能只出现一次。

## 实例分析

### 例句 1（考研阅读 — 比较省略 + gapping）

> Public universities serve a broader demographic than private ones _, and their graduates earn, on average, less than those from elite institutions _ , though **not significantly so**.
> — *改编自考研阅读*

**还原省略：**
```
...than private ones [serve] ← gapping：省略 serve (a broader demographic)
...than those from elite institutions [earn] ← 省略 earn
though not significantly so ← so = less（替代词）; 完整 = though they do not earn significantly less
```

---

### 例句 2（考研翻译 — 多重省略叠加）

> The reform improved efficiency more than **had been anticipated**, yet benefited the wealthy far more than _ the poor.
> — *改编自考研翻译*

**还原：**
```
more than [it] had been anticipated [that it would improve efficiency]
  ← 主语 it 省略 + 不定式内容省略

far more than [it benefited] the poor
  ← gapping：省略主语 it + 动词 benefited
```

**中文对照：** 改革提高效率的程度超出了预期，但对富人的好处远大于对穷人的好处。

---

### 例句 3（学术风格 — 共享 + VP 省略）

> The study neither confirmed nor refuted the original hypothesis, but it did suggest — as several earlier investigations had _ — that the relationship between the variables was more nuanced than previously thought _.
> — *改编自学术论文*

**还原：**
```
neither confirmed nor refuted [the original hypothesis]
  ← 共享结构：两个动词共享同一个宾语

as several earlier investigations had [suggested]
  ← VP 省略：had 后省略 suggested

more nuanced than [it was] previously thought [to be nuanced]
  ← 比较省略：省略主语 + be + 补语
```

## 自检

> [!example] 找出所有省略，还原完整形式，并标注省略类型：
> The government could have intervened earlier but chose not to. Critics argue that it should have, and more decisively than it ultimately did. The private sector adapted faster than the public one, investing more in innovation than the state was willing to, yet generating fewer jobs than economists had predicted it would.

> [!success]- 参考答案
>
> | # | 省略形式 | 还原 | 类型 |
> |---|---------|------|------|
> | ① | chose not **to** _ | chose not to **intervene** | 不定式省略 |
> | ② | it **should have** _ | it should have **intervened earlier** | VP 省略 |
> | ③ | more decisively than it ultimately **did** _ | than it ultimately **did intervene** | VP 省略（did 替代） |
> | ④ | faster than the public **one** _ | than the public **sector adapted** | 名词替代(one=sector) + gapping |
> | ⑤ | more...than the state was willing **to** _ | than the state was willing to **invest** | 不定式省略 |
> | ⑥ | fewer jobs than economists had predicted it **would** _ | than economists had predicted it would **generate** | VP 省略（would 后） |
