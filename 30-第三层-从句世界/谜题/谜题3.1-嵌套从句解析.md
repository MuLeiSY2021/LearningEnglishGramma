# 谜题 3.1: 嵌套从句解析

## 原文

以下每个句子都包含**多层嵌套从句**——句子里套句子，句子里再套句子。你的任务是把这个"俄罗斯套娃"拆开，画出每一层的嵌套结构。

> **A.** "The researchers who published the study that challenged the theory which had dominated the field for decades now believe that their initial conclusions, although largely correct, need to be revised in light of new evidence that has recently emerged."
> — *改编自 Nature*

> **B.** "Officials confirmed that the bridge, which was built in 1965, will be closed until engineers determine whether the cracks that appeared after the earthquake pose a structural risk."
> — *改编自 BBC News*

> **C.** "What surprised everyone was that the candidate who had been trailing in the polls managed to win the election because voters believed that she understood the issues that mattered most to them, even though her opponent had far more experience."
> — *改编自 The Guardian*

---

## 任务

> [!question] 你的任务：
> 1. 找出每句中的**所有从句**（主句只有一个，其余都是从句）
> 2. 用缩进画出嵌套结构（哪个从句包含在哪个从句内部）
> 3. 给每个从句标注**类型**（关系从句/名词从句/状语从句）和**功能**（修饰什么？充当什么成分？）

---

## 提示

> [!hint]- 提示 1（轻）
> 先找主句的主动词——它不在任何 that/who/which/because/although 等引导词后面。句 A 的主动词是 believe，句 B 的主动词是 confirmed，句 C 稍难——主句是"What... was that..."结构。

> [!hint]- 提示 2（中）
> 画结构时从外向内：先标出主句，然后找直接附着在主句上的从句，再找从句里面嵌套的从句。
> - 句 A 中 "although largely correct" 是一个**插入的让步状语从句**（省略了主语和系动词），它嵌套在 that 名词从句内部。
> - 句 B 中 "whether..." 从句是 determine 的宾语，而 "that appeared after the earthquake" 又嵌套在 whether 从句内部。

> [!hint]- 提示 3（重）
> 句 A 的完整结构：
> ```
> 主句：The researchers ... believe [that-从句]
>   ├── who published the study [that-从句]  ← 关系从句修饰 researchers
>   │     └── that challenged the theory [which-从句]  ← 关系从句修饰 study
>   │           └── which had dominated the field for decades  ← 关系从句修饰 theory
>   └── that their conclusions need to be revised ...  ← 名词从句(believe 的宾语)
>         ├── although largely correct  ← 状语从句（让步，省略形式）
>         └── that has recently emerged  ← 关系从句修饰 evidence
> ```

## 解答

> [!success]- 完整解析
>
> ### 句 A
>
> **主句：** The researchers ... now believe ...
>
> **括号标注法：**
> The researchers [**who** published the study [**that** challenged the theory [**which** had dominated the field for decades]]] now believe [**that** their initial conclusions, [**although** largely correct], need to be revised in light of new evidence [**that** has recently emerged]].
>
> **缩进结构图：**
> ```
> 主句：The researchers ... believe that-从句
> │
> ├─ [who published the study that...]  关系从句 → 修饰 researchers
> │    └─ [that challenged the theory which...]  关系从句 → 修饰 study
> │         └─ [which had dominated the field for decades]  关系从句 → 修饰 theory
> │
> └─ [that their conclusions ... need to be revised ...]  名词从句 → believe 的宾语
>      ├─ [although (they were) largely correct]  状语从句(让步) → 插入修饰 conclusions
>      └─ [that has recently emerged]  关系从句 → 修饰 evidence
> ```
>
> **从句清单（6个）：**
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | who published the study... | 关系从句 | 修饰 researchers |
> | that challenged the theory... | 关系从句 | 修饰 study |
> | which had dominated the field... | 关系从句 | 修饰 theory |
> | that their conclusions need... | 名词从句 | believe 的宾语 |
> | although largely correct | 状语从句(让步) | 修饰 conclusions 的判断 |
> | that has recently emerged | 关系从句 | 修饰 evidence |
>
> ---
>
> ### 句 B
>
> **主句：** Officials confirmed ...
>
> **括号标注法：**
> Officials confirmed [**that** the bridge, [**which** was built in 1965], will be closed [**until** engineers determine [**whether** the cracks [**that** appeared [**after** the earthquake]] pose a structural risk]]].
>
> **缩进结构图：**
> ```
> 主句：Officials confirmed that-从句
> │
> └─ [that the bridge ... will be closed until...]  名词从句 → confirmed 的宾语
>      ├─ [which was built in 1965]  关系从句(非限定性) → 修饰 bridge
>      └─ [until engineers determine whether...]  状语从句(时间) → closed 到什么时候
>           └─ [whether the cracks ... pose a structural risk]  名词从句 → determine 的宾语
>                └─ [that appeared after the earthquake]  关系从句 → 修饰 cracks
>                     └─ [after the earthquake]  介词短语(非从句)
> ```
>
> **从句清单（5个）：**
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | that the bridge will be closed... | 名词从句 | confirmed 的宾语 |
> | which was built in 1965 | 关系从句(非限定性) | 补充 bridge 信息 |
> | until engineers determine... | 状语从句(时间) | 修饰 will be closed |
> | whether the cracks pose... | 名词从句 | determine 的宾语 |
> | that appeared after the earthquake | 关系从句 | 修饰 cracks |
>
> ---
>
> ### 句 C
>
> **主句：** What surprised everyone was that...  （主系表结构）
>
> **括号标注法：**
> [**What** surprised everyone] was [**that** the candidate [**who** had been trailing in the polls] managed to win the election [**because** voters believed [**that** she understood the issues [**that** mattered most to them]]], [**even though** her opponent had far more experience]].
>
> **缩进结构图：**
> ```
> 主句：[What-从句] was [that-从句]
> │
> ├─ [What surprised everyone]  名词从句 → 充当主语
> │
> └─ [that the candidate ... managed to win ...]  名词从句 → 充当表语
>      ├─ [who had been trailing in the polls]  关系从句 → 修饰 candidate
>      ├─ [because voters believed that...]  状语从句(原因) → 为什么 win
>      │    └─ [that she understood the issues that...]  名词从句 → believed 的宾语
>      │         └─ [that mattered most to them]  关系从句 → 修饰 issues
>      └─ [even though her opponent had far more experience]  状语从句(让步)
> ```
>
> **从句清单（7个）：**
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | What surprised everyone | 名词从句 | 主语 |
> | that the candidate managed to win... | 名词从句 | 表语 |
> | who had been trailing in the polls | 关系从句 | 修饰 candidate |
> | because voters believed... | 状语从句(原因) | 修饰 managed to win |
> | that she understood the issues... | 名词从句 | believed 的宾语 |
> | that mattered most to them | 关系从句 | 修饰 issues |
> | even though her opponent had far more experience | 状语从句(让步) | 修饰整个结果 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 总结规律
>
> **嵌套从句的阅读策略：**
> 1. 找到主句动词（不被任何引导词管辖的那个动词）
> 2. 主句动词前面的所有从句 = 修饰主语或充当主语
> 3. 主句动词后面的从句 = 宾语/表语/状语
> 4. 从句内部出现新引导词 = 下一层嵌套开始
>
> **涉及语法：**
> - [[关系从句]]
> - [[名词从句]]
> - [[状语从句]]
