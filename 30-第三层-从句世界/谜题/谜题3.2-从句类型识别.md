# 谜题 3.2: 从句类型识别

## 原文

以下每个句子都包含一个或多个从句。你的任务是找出每个从句并准确分类。

> **A.** "The world you see is merely a shadow of what truly exists."
> — *改编自 Plato / 游戏常见台词*

> **B.** "Although no one expected it, the underdog team won because they refused to give up."
> — *改编自 ESPN*

> **C.** "Scientists have discovered that the ocean, which covers 71% of the Earth's surface, contains species that have never been documented."
> — *改编自 National Geographic*

> **D.** "Whether we succeed or fail depends on how well we prepare."
> — *常见表达*

> **E.** "The knight, exhausted from battle, entered the castle where his ancestors had once ruled, hoping that peace would return to the land."
> — *改编自 fantasy literature*

> **F.** "If you stare into the abyss long enough, you'll find that the abyss has been staring back at you since the moment you arrived."
> — *改编自 Nietzsche / Baldur's Gate*

> **G.** "The fact that millions of people share the same vices does not make these vices virtues."
> — *Erich Fromm*

> **H.** "Provided that the servers remain stable, the update which players have been requesting since launch will go live tomorrow."
> — *改编自 game patch notes*

---

## 任务

> [!question] 你的任务：
> 1. 找出每句中的**所有从句**（包括缩减从句）
> 2. 分类每个从句：
>    - 关系从句（限定性 / 非限定性）
>    - 名词从句（主语 / 宾语 / 表语 / 同位语）
>    - 状语从句（时间 / 原因 / 条件 / 让步 / 目的 / 结果）
>    - 缩减从句（由什么从句缩减而来）
> 3. 说明每个从句的**功能**（修饰什么？充当什么？）

---

## 提示

> [!hint]- 提示 1（轻）
> 并非所有从句都有明显的引导词！句 A 中有一个 contact clause（关系代词被省略了），句 E 中有缩减形式（分词短语）。

> [!hint]- 提示 2（中）
> 名词从句 vs 关系从句的判断关键：
> - "the fact **that**..." 中的 that = 同位语从句（从句解释 fact 的内容，不是修饰它）
> - "the species **that**..." 中的 that = 关系从句（that 在从句中充当主语或宾语）
> 
> 测试方法：从句里缺成分 → 关系从句；从句完整 → 名词从句

> [!hint]- 提示 3（重）
> 句 E 的完整分析：
> - "exhausted from battle" = 缩减从句（who was exhausted from battle）
> - "where his ancestors had once ruled" = 关系从句修饰 castle
> - "hoping that peace would return" = 缩减从句（现在分词，表伴随）
> - "that peace would return to the land" = 名词从句（hoping 的宾语）

## 解答

> [!success]- 完整解析
>
> ### 句 A
> "The world (that) you see is merely a shadow of what truly exists."
>
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | (that) you see | 关系从句(限定性，contact clause) | 修饰 world；that 省略，作 see 的宾语 |
> | what truly exists | 名词从句 | 介词 of 的宾语（= the thing that truly exists） |
>
> ---
>
> ### 句 B
> "Although no one expected it, the underdog team won because they refused to give up."
>
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | Although no one expected it | 状语从句(让步) | 前置，有逗号 |
> | because they refused to give up | 状语从句(原因) | 后置，解释为什么赢 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 句 C
> "Scientists have discovered that the ocean, which covers 71% of the Earth's surface, contains species that have never been documented."
>
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | that the ocean ... contains species... | 名词从句 | discovered 的宾语 |
> | which covers 71% of the Earth's surface | 关系从句(非限定性) | 补充 ocean 的信息 |
> | that have never been documented | 关系从句(限定性) | 修饰 species；that 作主语 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 句 D
> "Whether we succeed or fail depends on how well we prepare."
>
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | Whether we succeed or fail | 名词从句(主语) | 充当整句的主语 |
> | how well we prepare | 名词从句 | 介词 on 的宾语 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 句 E
> "The knight, exhausted from battle, entered the castle where his ancestors had once ruled, hoping that peace would return to the land."
>
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | exhausted from battle | 缩减从句 (← who was exhausted from battle) | 非限定性，修饰 knight |
> | where his ancestors had once ruled | 关系从句(限定性) | 修饰 castle |
> | hoping that peace would return... | 缩减从句 (← 现在分词短语，表伴随动作) | 修饰主语 knight |
> | that peace would return to the land | 名词从句 | hoping 的宾语 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 句 F
> "If you stare into the abyss long enough, you'll find that the abyss has been staring back at you since the moment you arrived."
>
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | If you stare into the abyss long enough | 状语从句(条件) | 前置条件 |
> | that the abyss has been staring back... | 名词从句 | find 的宾语 |
> | (that) you arrived | 关系从句(限定性，contact clause) | 修饰 the moment；that 省略 |
>
> 注意："since the moment" 中 since 是介词（不是从句引导词），后面跟名词短语 "the moment (that) you arrived"。
>
> ---
>
> ### 句 G
> "The fact that millions of people share the same vices does not make these vices virtues."
>
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | that millions of people share the same vices | 名词从句(同位语) | 解释 the fact 的具体内容 |
>
> **辨析关键：** 这不是关系从句！测试：从句内部完整（millions share vices —— 不缺任何成分），所以 that 是连接词，引导同位语从句。如果是关系从句，从句内部应该缺一个名词位置。
>
> ---
>
> ### 句 H
> "Provided that the servers remain stable, the update which players have been requesting since launch will go live tomorrow."
>
> | 从句 | 类型 | 功能 |
> |------|------|------|
> | Provided that the servers remain stable | 状语从句(条件) | 前置条件（= if） |
> | which players have been requesting since launch | 关系从句(限定性) | 修饰 update；which 作 requesting 的宾语 |
>
> **注意：** 虽然 which 通常用于非限定性从句，但这里没有逗号，且语义上是限定性的（特指那个玩家一直在要求的更新）。在现代英语中，限定性从句更常用 that，但 which 也不算错误。
>
