# 从句缩减 (Reduced Clauses)

## 直觉入口

> [!question] 每组中 A 和 B 意思相同——B 是如何"压缩"A 的？去掉了什么？
>
> **第一组（关系从句 → ?）：**
> A. The man **who is standing** at the door is my uncle.
> B. The man **standing** at the door is my uncle.
>
> **第二组（关系从句 → ?）：**
> A. The paper **which was published** in Nature attracted attention.
> B. The paper **published** in Nature attracted attention.
>
> **第三组（状语从句 → ?）：**
> A. **Because she was exhausted**, she fell asleep immediately.
> B. **Exhausted**, she fell asleep immediately.
>
> **第四组（状语从句 → ?）：**
> A. **After he had finished** the report, he submitted it.
> B. **Having finished** the report, he submitted it.

试着回答：
1. 从 A 到 B，每次都去掉了哪些词？（关系代词？连词？be 动词？主语？）
2. 留下的部分是什么形式？（-ing？-ed？to do？）
3. 缩减后，怎么判断原来的从句是什么类型？

## 中文桥梁

**中文怎么做：**

中文不存在"从句缩减"的概念——因为中文本来就不需要关系代词和连词来标记从句：

| 英语完整从句 | 英语缩减后 | 中文 |
|-------------|-----------|------|
| the man **who is** standing there | the man standing there | 站在那里的人 |
| **Because she was** tired, she left | Tired, she left | 她累了，就走了 |

**关键差异：**
- 英语的"缩减"其实是让句子变得更像中文——去掉那些中文本来就没有的标记词。
- 但中文读者的困难在于：缩减后的英语句子少了明确的信号词（who/which/because/after），必须靠位置和形式推断原来的关系。

**为什么英语要缩减：**

学术写作追求信息密度——用最少的词装最多的信息。从句缩减是英语"信息压缩"的核心工具。考研长难句几乎每句都有缩减，不会还原 = 读不懂。

## 规则揭示

> [!tip]- 展开核心规则
>
> ### 一、关系从句缩减（后置定语）
>
> **通用步骤：** 去掉关系代词 + be 动词，保留剩余部分。
>
> | 原始关系从句 | 缩减结果 | 条件 |
> |-------------|---------|------|
> | who/which + **is doing** | → **doing** | 主动进行 |
> | who/which + **is done** | → **done** | 被动 |
> | who/which + **is** + adj/prep | → **adj/prep** | 系表结构 |
> | who/which + **does** (非 be) | → **doing** | 主动一般（需变 -ing） |
> | the first/only... + who + **did** | → **to do** | 序数词/最高级触发 |
>
> **完整示例：**
>
> | 完整形式 | 缩减形式 |
> |---------|---------|
> | students **who are studying** abroad | students **studying** abroad |
> | a letter **which was written** in 1945 | a letter **written** in 1945 |
> | the man **who is responsible** for this | the man **responsible** for this |
> | anyone **who wishes** to apply | anyone **wishing** to apply |
> | the first person **who solved** it | the first person **to solve** it |
>
> ---
>
> ### 二、状语从句缩减
>
> **条件：** 从句主语 = 主句主语（否则不能缩减，除非用独立主格）
>
> **步骤：** 去掉从句主语 + 可选去掉连词 + 动词变非谓语形式。
>
> | 原始状语从句 | 缩减结果 | 保留连词？ |
> |-------------|---------|-----------|
> | **Because** she **was** tired, she... | **Tired**, she... | 去掉 |
> | **Because** she **didn't know** the answer, she... | **Not knowing** the answer, she... | 去掉 |
> | **After** he **had finished**, he... | **Having finished**, he... | 去掉 |
> | **While** she **was walking**, she... | **While walking**, she... | 保留 while |
> | **Although** he **was** injured, he... | **Although injured**, he... | 保留 although |
> | **If** it **is** heated, metal... | **If heated**, metal... | 保留 if |
>
> **规律：**
> - 时间/原因连词（because, after, before）缩减后通常**去掉**
> - 让步/条件连词（although, while, if, unless）缩减后通常**保留**（否则语义不明）
>
> ---
>
> ### 三、缩减的方向判断（-ing vs -ed）
>
> | 逻辑关系 | 使用形式 | 判断依据 |
> |---------|---------|---------|
> | 主语**主动**发出动作 | V-ing | 主语是动作的执行者 |
> | 主语**被动**承受动作 | V-ed | 主语是动作的承受者 |
> | 动作**先于**主句发生 | having done / having been done | 时间先后关系 |
>
> **快速判断：** 把缩减后的分词和主句主语配对——
> - The man **standing** there → the man is standing ✓（主动）
> - The report **published** last week → the report was published ✓（被动）
>
> ---
>
> ### 四、还原练习法（考研核心技能）
>
> 遇到缩减结构时的三步还原：
>
> 1. **找到非谓语动词**（-ing/-ed/to do）
> 2. **确定逻辑主语**（通常 = 最近的名词 或 主句主语）
> 3. **还原为完整从句**：
>    - 后置的 → 加 who/which + 恢复时态
>    - 句首/句末的 → 加 because/when/although + 恢复主语和时态
>
> | 缩减形式 | 还原 |
> |---------|------|
> | the data **collected** over five years | the data **which was collected** over five years |
> | **Hoping** to impress his boss, Tom... | **Because Tom hoped** to impress his boss, Tom... |
> | **Not satisfied** with the results, they... | **Because they were not satisfied**... |
> | **Once established**, the system... | **Once it was established**... |
>
> ---
>
> ### 五、不能缩减的情况
>
> | 情况 | 例句 | 原因 |
> |------|------|------|
> | 关系从句中关系词做宾语 | the book **that I read** | 缩减后无法保留 I（不同主语） |
> | 从句用一般现在表状态 | the house **that stands** on the hill | stands → standing 改变了时态含义（进行 vs 一般）⚠️ |
> | 状语从句主语 ≠ 主句主语 | **Because the weather was bad**, we stayed in. | 不能缩为 *Being bad, we...（主语不匹配） |
>
> 第三种情况的解决办法：用独立主格 → **The weather being bad**, we stayed in. 详见 [[非谓语动词]]。

## 实例分析

### 例句 1（考研阅读 — 多重缩减叠加）

> Researchers **working at** the intersection of neuroscience and education have proposed a model **designed to predict** learning outcomes, **drawing on** data **gathered from** thousands of students **enrolled in** online courses.
> — *改编自考研阅读*

**还原所有缩减：**
```
Researchers [who are working at...]           ← ① 关系从句缩减（-ing 主动）
have proposed a model [which was designed to predict...]  ← ② 关系从句缩减（-ed 被动）
[as they drew on / drawing on] data           ← ③ 状语从句缩减（-ing 伴随/方式）
  [which was gathered from] thousands of students  ← ④ 关系从句缩减（-ed 被动）
    [who are enrolled in] online courses      ← ⑤ 关系从句缩减（-ed 被动）
```

**一句话中 5 个缩减！** 这就是考研长难句的典型密度。如果全部还原成完整从句，句子会变成三行——但原文只有两行。

---

### 例句 2（考研翻译 — 状语从句缩减 + 连词保留）

> **Although initially dismissed as impractical**, the theory **proposed by** the young researcher eventually proved correct, **fundamentally altering** our understanding of gene regulation.
> — *改编自考研翻译*

**还原：**
```
[Although it was initially dismissed as impractical],  ← 状语从句缩减（保留 although）
the theory [which was proposed by the young researcher]  ← 关系从句缩减（-ed 被动）
eventually proved correct,
[and it fundamentally altered...]  ← 状语从句缩减（-ing 结果/伴随）
```

**中文对照：** 尽管最初被视为不切实际，这位年轻研究者提出的理论最终被证明是正确的，从根本上改变了我们对基因调控的理解。

---

### 例句 3（学术风格 — having done + 独立主格）

> **Having analyzed** the data **collected** during the first phase, and **the initial hypotheses having been confirmed**, the team proceeded to design a larger trial **involving** over 5,000 participants **selected from** diverse demographic backgrounds.
> — *改编自 The Lancet*

**还原：**
```
[After they had analyzed] the data [which had been collected] during the first phase,
  ← having done 缩减（时间先后）    ← 关系从句缩减（-ed 被动）

and [after the initial hypotheses had been confirmed],  ← 独立主格（主语 ≠ the team）
the team proceeded to design a larger trial
  [which involves/involved] over 5,000 participants   ← 关系从句缩减（-ing 主动）
    [who were selected from] diverse backgrounds      ← 关系从句缩减（-ed 被动）
```

## 自检

> [!example] 找出所有缩减结构，还原为完整从句，并标注缩减类型：
> Confronted with evidence suggesting that policies implemented without adequate consultation tend to fail, the minister, once considered a rising star within the party, acknowledged the need for a revised approach incorporating input from stakeholders previously excluded from the decision-making process.

> [!success]- 参考答案
>
> **共 7 个缩减结构：**
>
> | # | 缩减形式 | 还原 | 类型 |
> |---|---------|------|------|
> | ① | **Confronted with** evidence | Because he/she was confronted with evidence | 状语从句缩减（-ed 被动，原因） |
> | ② | evidence **suggesting** that... | evidence **which suggests** that... | 关系从句缩减（-ing 主动） |
> | ③ | policies **implemented** without... | policies **which were implemented** without... | 关系从句缩减（-ed 被动） |
> | ④ | **once considered** a rising star | **who was once considered** a rising star | 关系从句缩减（-ed 被动，插入语） |
> | ⑤ | a **revised** approach | an approach **which has been revised** | 关系从句缩减（-ed 被动，前置单词） |
> | ⑥ | approach **incorporating** input | approach **which incorporates** input | 关系从句缩减（-ing 主动） |
> | ⑦ | stakeholders **previously excluded** from... | stakeholders **who were previously excluded** from... | 关系从句缩减（-ed 被动） |
>
> **注意 ⑤：** 单个过去分词可以前置（a revised approach），但分词短语必须后置（an approach revised by the committee）。
