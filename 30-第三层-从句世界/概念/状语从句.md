# 状语从句 (Adverbial Clauses)

## 直觉入口

> [!question] 观察这些句子，找出"可选的上下文"——删掉它句子依然成立，但逻辑关系会消失。它表达的是什么关系？
>
> **A.** **Before the sun rose**, the army had already crossed the river.
>
> **B.** She kept running **even though her legs were burning**.
>
> **C.** **If you touch that wire**, you will get shocked.
>
> **D.** He spoke slowly **so that everyone could understand**.
>
> **E.** The bridge collapsed **because the foundations had been weakened by decades of erosion**.

试着回答：
1. 每句中哪个部分是"可以删掉的上下文从句"？
2. 每个上下文从句和主句之间是什么逻辑关系？（时间？原因？条件？让步？目的？）
3. 每个从句开头的词（before/even though/if/so that/because）在做什么？

> [!note]- 我的假设
> *(写下你的分类——你发现了几种逻辑关系？)*

## 中文桥梁

**中文怎么做：**

中文表达同样的逻辑关系时，经常**不用连接词**——靠语序和语境让读者自己"意会"：

| 英语（必须标记） | 中文（常省略标记） |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Because** it's cold, wear a jacket. | 天冷了，穿外套吧。 |
| **Although** he's rich, he's unhappy. | 他很有钱，但不开心。/ 他那么有钱，不开心。 |
| **After** I eat, I'll go. | 我吃完饭就走。 |
| **If** you don't hurry, we'll be late. | 你不快点，我们要迟到了。 |

**核心问题：** 中文是"意合"语言——逻辑关系藏在语序里，读者自动感知。英语是"形合"语言——**每一层逻辑关系都必须用一个明确的词标记出来**。

这就是为什么你看英文长句觉得"连接词太多"——它们不是增加了复杂度，只是把中文读者已经无意识感知到的逻辑关系**显性化**了。

> 详见 [[意合与形合]]

## 规则揭示

> [!tip]- 展开规则
>
> ### 一、按功能分类的引导词总表
>
> | 逻辑关系 | 常用引导词 | 例句 |
> |---------|-----------|------|
> | **时间** | when, while, before, after, since, until, as soon as, once, by the time | **Once** you see it, you can't unsee it. |
> | **原因** | because, since, as, now that, given that, seeing that | He quit **because** the pay was terrible. |
> | **条件** | if, unless, provided (that), as long as, in case, on condition that | **Unless** you apologize, I'm leaving. |
> | **让步** | although, though, even though, while, whereas, no matter how | **Even though** he trained hard, he lost. |
> | **目的** | so that, in order that, lest | She whispered **so that** no one would hear. |
> | **结果** | so...that, such...that | It was **so** cold **that** the pipes froze. |
> | **方式** | as, as if, as though | He acted **as if** nothing had happened. |
> | **比较** | than, as...as | She runs faster **than** I do. |
>
> ---
>
> ### 二、位置与逗号规则
>
> 状语从句的位置非常灵活——可以放在主句前面或后面：
>
> | 位置 | 逗号 | 效果 |
> |------|------|------|
> | **前置**（从句在前） | 需要逗号 | **Because it was late,** we went home. |
> | **后置**（从句在后） | 通常不需要 | We went home **because it was late**. |
>
> **前置 = 设置背景/条件，然后再说主要信息。**
> **后置 = 先说主要信息，再补充原因/条件等。**
>
> 学术写作中经常把时间/条件从句**前置**来设定语境：
> - *After the participants completed the survey, the researchers analyzed the data.*
>
> ---
>
> ### 三、易混淆辨析
>
> **because vs since vs as（原因）：**
> - because：强调原因本身（回答 why？）
> - since/as：原因已知，重点在结果（"既然..."）
>
> **although vs while vs whereas（让步/对比）：**
> - although/though/even though：让步（承认A，但B）
> - while/whereas：对比两个平行事实（A如此，B如此）
>
> **if vs unless vs provided that（条件）：**
> - if：如果X，那么Y
> - unless = if...not：除非X，否则Y
> - provided that：只要X（比 if 更强调前提条件）
>
> ---
>
> ### 四、so...that 与 such...that（结果从句）
>
> ```
> so + 形容词/副词 + that...
>   He was so tired that he fell asleep immediately.
>
> such + (a/an) + 名词 + that...
>   It was such a boring movie that I left halfway through.
> ```
>
> 注意：这里的 that 不是关系代词，是结果标记词。见 [[that的多重身份]]。

## 实例分析

### 例句 1（状语从句堆叠——学术文）

> **Although** the initial results were promising, the team decided to delay publication **until** they could replicate the findings, **because** previous studies in the field had been retracted **after** errors were discovered.

**拆解——四个状语从句套在一个主句上：**
```
主句：the team decided to delay publication

状语从句层级：
├── Although the initial results were promising  ← 让步（前置，有逗号）
├── until they could replicate the findings      ← 时间（后置，delay 到什么时候）
├── because previous studies had been retracted  ← 原因（为什么要 delay）
│     └── after errors were discovered           ← 时间（嵌套在 because 从句内）
```

**中文意合版本：** 初步结果不错，但团队还是推迟发表了。他们想再验证一遍。这个领域以前有论文因为发现错误被撤稿了。

看到了吗？中文用四个短句，逻辑全靠读者自己串联。英文用 although/until/because/after 把每层关系标得明明白白。

---

### 例句 2（条件从句——游戏）

> "**If** you peer into the darkness long enough, **even if** the darkness does not peer back, you may find something **that** was not meant to be found."
> — *改编自 Bloodborne*

**拆解：**
```
主句：you may find something [that was not meant to be found]
                              └── 关系从句修饰 something

├── If you peer into the darkness long enough  ← 条件
├── even if the darkness does not peer back    ← 让步条件（即使...也）
```

注意 even if 表达的是"退一步的条件"——即使最坏情况（黑暗没有回望你），主句仍成立。

---

### 例句 3（目的 + 结果——日常）

> She practiced every day **so that** she could pass the exam, and she worked **so** hard **that** her teacher was impressed.

**拆解：**
```
句子1：She practiced every day [so that she could pass the exam]
                                └── 目的状语从句（为了什么）

句子2：she worked so hard [that her teacher was impressed]
                          └── 结果状语从句（到什么程度）
```

**辨析 so that vs so...that：**
- so that + 从句 = 目的（"为了..."）
- so + 形容词/副词 + that + 从句 = 结果（"如此...以至于..."）

## 自检

> [!example] 找出所有状语从句，标注逻辑类型和位置：
> Although many experts predicted that the market would crash if interest rates continued to rise, the economy remained stable because consumer spending stayed strong, even though inflation had reached levels not seen since the 1980s.

> [!success]- 参考答案
>
> ```
> 主句：the economy remained stable
>
> ├── Although many experts predicted that...  ← 让步（前置）
> │     └── that the market would crash        ← 名词从句（不是状语从句！是 predicted 的宾语）
> │           └── if interest rates continued to rise  ← 条件（嵌套在名词从句内）
> ├── because consumer spending stayed strong  ← 原因（后置）
> └── even though inflation had reached levels...  ← 让步（后置）
> ```
>
> | 从句 | 类型 | 位置 | 逗号 |
> |------|------|------|------|
> | Although many experts predicted... | 让步 | 前置 | 有 |
> | if interest rates continued to rise | 条件 | 嵌套 | — |
> | because consumer spending stayed strong | 原因 | 后置 | 无 |
> | even though inflation had reached... | 让步 | 后置 | 有（补充性） |
>
> **陷阱：** "that the market would crash" 不是状语从句，而是名词从句（predicted 的宾语）。不要看到 that 就以为是同一种结构！
