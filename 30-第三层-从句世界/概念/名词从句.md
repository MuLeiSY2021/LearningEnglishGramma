# 名词从句 (Noun Clauses)

## 直觉入口

> [!question] 观察这些句子——加粗部分在主句中扮演什么角色？（主语？宾语？其他？）
>
> **A.** **That the earth is round** is now common knowledge.
>
> **B.** Scientists discovered **that the virus had mutated**.
>
> **C.** The question is **whether we can afford the risk**.
>
> **D.** The fact **that language shapes thought** remains controversial.
>
> **E.** **What surprised everyone** was his calm reaction.

试着回答：
1. 每个加粗部分本身都是一个完整句子吗？
2. 它们在主句中充当什么成分？（提示：试着用 "it" 或 "something" 替换）
3. D 句中的 that 从句和 B 句中的 that 从句有什么不同？

> [!note]- 我的假设
> *(D 句的 that 从句是在解释 fact 的内容吗？还是修饰 fact？它和关系从句有什么区别？)*

## 中文桥梁

**中文怎么做：**

中文直接把一个句子放在名词位置，不需要任何连接词：

| 英语（需要引导词） | 中文（直接放入） |
|-------------------|----------------|
| I know **that he is lying**. | 我知道**他在撒谎**。 |
| **That he left** shocked us. | **他走了**让我们震惊。 |
| The news **that war broke out** | **战争爆发的**消息 |

**关键差异：**
- 中文：句子直接做名词用，"他在撒谎"既可以独立成句，也可以直接做"知道"的宾语，形式不变。
- 英语：从句必须有引导词（that/whether/what/how...）作为"信号"，告诉读者"接下来是一个从句，不是主句"。

**为什么英语需要引导词：**

英语语序相对固定（SVO），如果两个句子直接拼在一起（*I know he is lying* 在口语中可以但正式文体不行），读者很难判断句子边界在哪里。引导词就像一个"从句开始"的标志牌。

## 规则揭示

> [!tip]- 展开核心规则
>
> ### 一、名词从句的四种位置
>
> | 位置 | 名称 | 例句 | 可替换为 |
> |------|------|------|---------|
> | 主语 | 主语从句 | **That he resigned** surprised everyone. | **It** surprised everyone. |
> | 宾语 | 宾语从句 | I believe **that honesty matters**. | I believe **it**. |
> | 表语 | 表语从句 | The problem is **that we lack data**. | The problem is **this**. |
> | 同位语 | 同位语从句 | The idea **that time is relative** changed physics. | The idea **itself** |
>
> **判断方法：** 把从句替换成 it/this/something——如果句子仍然合法，说明从句处于名词位置。
>
> ---
>
> ### 二、引导词选择
>
> | 引导词 | 从句中的角色 | 是否有实际含义 | 例句 |
> |--------|-------------|--------------|------|
> | **that** | 无角色（纯标记） | 无 | I know **that** she left. |
> | **whether/if** | 无角色 | 表"是否" | I wonder **whether** she left. |
> | **what** | 主语/宾语/表语 | "…的东西/事情" | **What** he said was true. |
> | **who/whom** | 主语/宾语 | "…的人" | I don't know **who** called. |
> | **which** | 定语/主语/宾语 | "哪一个" | Tell me **which** you prefer. |
> | **when/where/how/why** | 状语 | 时间/地点/方式/原因 | I remember **where** we met. |
>
> **that vs what：**
> - that 只是标记，从句内部是完整句：I know **that** [she is honest].
> - what = the thing that，从句内部缺成分：I know **what** [she said ___]. (what = said 的宾语)
>
> ---
>
> ### 三、同位语从句 vs 关系从句（考研核心辨析！）
>
> 两者都跟在名词后面，都可以用 that 引导，但本质不同：
>
> | | 同位语从句 | 关系从句 |
> |--|-----------|---------|
> | 功能 | 解释名词的**内容** | 修饰/限定名词 |
> | that 的角色 | 纯连接词（无成分） | 关系代词（做主语/宾语） |
> | 从句是否完整 | **完整**（不缺成分） | **不完整**（缺主语或宾语） |
> | 能换成 which 吗 | **不能** | 能 |
> | 常搭配的名词 | fact, idea, news, belief, hope, evidence, conclusion | 任何名词 |
>
> **对比：**
> - The news **that he had won** excited us. → 同位语（从句完整，解释 news 的内容是什么）
> - The news **that he told me** was false. → 关系从句（从句缺宾语：he told me ___，that = 宾语）
>
> **快速判断法：** 在 that 后面的从句里找——有没有"空位"（缺的成分）？
> - 有空位 → 关系从句（that 填补了那个空位）
> - 没空位 → 同位语从句（that 只是标记）
>
> ---
>
> ### 四、形式主语 It（主语从句前移）
>
> 当主语从句太长时，英语倾向于用 it 占位，把真正的从句移到句尾：
>
> | 头重（不自然） | It 前移（自然） |
> |---------------|----------------|
> | **That she survived** is a miracle. | **It** is a miracle **that she survived**. |
> | **Whether he will come** is uncertain. | **It** is uncertain **whether he will come**. |
>
> > 详见 [[外置]]
>
> ⚠️ **辨析陷阱：** It is...that 可能是：
> - 形式主语：It is obvious **that** he lied.（= That he lied is obvious）
> - 强调句：It is **Tom** that broke the window.（强调 Tom）
>
> 判断方法：去掉 It is...that，剩余部分能否构成完整句？
> - ✅ Tom broke the window. → 强调句
> - ❌ *Obvious he lied. → 不是强调句，是形式主语
>
> > 详见 [[分裂句]]
>
> ---
>
> ### 五、宾语从句中 that 的省略
>
> 在口语和非正式写作中，宾语从句的 that 常省略：
>
> - I think ~~that~~ she's right. ✓
> - I believe ~~that~~ we can win. ✓
>
> **不能省略的情况：**
> - 主语从句：**That** he left is obvious.（不能省 that）
> - 同位语从句：The fact **that** she resigned...（不能省）
> - 两个并列宾语从句中的第二个：He said (that) he was tired and **that** he wanted to leave.（第二个 that 不能省，否则歧义）

## 实例分析

### 例句 1（考研翻译 — 主语从句 + 形式主语）

> **It** has long been assumed **that children learn their first language primarily through imitation**, but recent research suggests **that the process is far more complex than mere copying**.
> — *改编自考研翻译*

**拆解：**
```
It has long been assumed [that children learn... through imitation],
  形式主语 it              └── 真正主语（that 从句），完整句，that 无成分

but recent research suggests [that the process is far more complex...],
                              └── 宾语从句（suggests 的宾语）
                                  than mere copying ← 比较从句省略
```

**中文对照：** 长期以来人们一直假设儿童主要通过模仿学习母语，但最新研究表明这一过程远比单纯模仿复杂得多。

---

### 例句 2（考研阅读 — 同位语从句 vs 关系从句共现）

> The conclusion **that economic growth inevitably leads to environmental degradation** — a view **that dominated policy circles for decades** — is now being challenged by evidence **that sustainable development is achievable**.
> — *改编自考研阅读*

**拆解：**
```
The conclusion [that economic growth inevitably leads to...]  ← ① 同位语从句
                从句完整，解释 conclusion 的内容

— a view [that dominated policy circles for decades] —        ← ② 关系从句
           从句缺主语（that = 主语 = a view），修饰 view

is now being challenged by evidence [that sustainable development is achievable]
                                     ← ③ 同位语从句
                                     从句完整，解释 evidence 的内容
```

**辨析关键：**
- ① that 后面 "economic growth leads to..." 是完整句 → 同位语
- ② that 后面 "___ dominated policy circles" 缺主语 → 关系从句
- ③ that 后面 "sustainable development is achievable" 完整 → 同位语

---

### 例句 3（考研完形 — what 从句做主语）

> **What distinguishes successful companies from failing ones** is not merely the quality of their products but **how effectively they adapt to changing market conditions**.
> — *改编自考研完形*

**拆解：**
```
[What distinguishes successful companies from failing ones]  ← what 主语从句
  what = distinguishes 的主语（= The thing that distinguishes...）

is

not merely the quality... but [how effectively they adapt...]  ← how 表语从句
                                how = 方式
```

**中文对照：** 区分成功公司与失败公司的不仅仅是产品质量，而是它们适应不断变化的市场条件的效率。

---

### 例句 4（学术风格 — 多层名词从句嵌套）

> The researchers acknowledged **that whether the findings could be replicated in different cultural contexts remained to be seen**, but maintained **that what they had demonstrated was sufficient to warrant further investigation**.
> — *改编自学术论文*

**拆解：**
```
The researchers acknowledged [that ... remained to be seen],
                              └── 宾语从句
  内部：[whether the findings could be replicated...] remained...
         └── whether 从句做 remained 的主语（主语从句嵌套在宾语从句中）

but maintained [that what they had demonstrated was sufficient...],
                └── 宾语从句
  内部：[what they had demonstrated] was sufficient...
         └── what 从句做 was 的主语（又一个嵌套主语从句）
```

**中文对照：** 研究人员承认这些发现能否在不同文化背景下被重复验证尚有待观察，但坚持认为他们所证明的东西足以值得进一步研究。

## 自检

> [!example] 找出所有名词从句，判断类型（主语/宾语/表语/同位语），并辨析 that 的身份：
> It is widely believed that the assumption that intelligence is fixed — an idea that has persisted since the early 20th century — overlooks what decades of research have shown: that the brain remains plastic throughout life.

> [!success]- 参考答案
>
> **共 5 个名词从句 + 1 个关系从句：**
>
> | # | 从句 | 类型 | that 的身份 |
> |---|------|------|------------|
> | ① | that the assumption... overlooks... | 主语从句（it 是形式主语） | 连接词（无成分） |
> | ② | that intelligence is fixed | 同位语从句（解释 assumption 的内容） | 连接词（无成分） |
> | ③ | that has persisted since... | **关系从句**（修饰 idea，缺主语） | **关系代词**（= 主语） |
> | ④ | what decades of research have shown | 宾语从句（overlooks 的宾语） | 无 that（用 what） |
> | ⑤ | that the brain remains plastic... | 同位语从句（解释冒号前 what 的具体内容） | 连接词（无成分） |
>
> **辨析关键：**
> - ② that intelligence is fixed → 从句完整，解释 assumption 是什么 → 同位语
> - ③ that has persisted → 从句缺主语（___ has persisted） → 关系从句
> - ④ what = the thing that（overlooks 的宾语 + shown 的宾语，双重角色）
