# 比较结构 (Comparative Structures)

## 直觉入口

> [!question] 观察这些句子——它们在"比"什么？省略了什么？
>
> **A.** The problem is **more complex than** it appears.
>
> **B.** She is **no more** a scientist **than** I am a poet.
>
> **C.** **The more** carefully you plan, **the fewer** mistakes you make.
>
> **D.** The country's GDP is now **three times as large as** it was a decade ago.
>
> **E.** He would **rather resign than** compromise his principles.

试着回答：
1. B 句到底是什么意思？她是科学家吗？我是诗人吗？
2. C 句中 the more...the more 的结构，为什么 the 后面跟比较级？
3. 每句中 than/as 后面是否省略了什么？试着补全。

> [!note]- 我的假设
> *(试着把 B 句翻译成中文——这句话到底在肯定还是否定？)*

---

## 中文桥梁

**中文怎么做：**

中文比较结构简单直接——用"比"字：

| 英语 | 中文 |
|------|------|
| She is taller **than** me. | 她**比**我高。 |
| He is **as** tall **as** his father. | 他**和**他爸**一样**高。 |
| **The more** you read, **the more** you know. | 读得**越**多，知道得**越**多。 |

**关键差异：**
- 中文"比"字句不需要省略——"她比我高"已经完整。
- 英语 than/as 后面其实是一个**从句**，只是大量省略了重复部分：She is taller than I *am tall* → 省略 am tall。
- 中文没有 no more...than 这种"双重否定式比较"，所以这类结构对中文母语者极难理解。

**为什么英语比较结构难：**

英语比较的本质是**两个从句的对照**，但为了简洁，大量省略重复信息。省略越多，句子越短，但理解难度越大。考研阅读就专挑省略最重的比较句来考。

## 规则揭示

> [!tip]- 展开核心规则
>
> ### 一、基本比较框架
>
> | 类型 | 结构 | 例句 |
> |------|------|------|
> | 等比 | as + adj/adv + **as** + 比较对象 | She is **as** smart **as** her sister. |
> | 差比（更） | more/adj-er + **than** + 比较对象 | This is **harder than** I expected. |
> | 差比（不如） | less + adj + **than** / not as...as | He is **less** experienced **than** his rival. |
> | 最高级 | the most/adj-est + 范围 | She is **the most** qualified candidate. |
>
> **核心认知：** than / as 后面是一个**省略的从句**。
> - This is harder than I expected **[it would be]**.
> - She runs faster than he **[runs]**.
>
> ---
>
> ### 二、倍数比较
>
> | 结构 | 例句 | 意思 |
> |------|------|------|
> | X times **as** + adj + **as** | The room is **three times as large as** mine. | 是我的三倍大 |
> | X times + **比较级** + than | The room is **three times larger than** mine. | 比我的大三倍（= 是四倍）⚠️ |
> | X times + **the** + 名词 | The room is **three times the size of** mine. | 是我的三倍大小 |
>
> ⚠️ "three times larger than" 在逻辑上有歧义（3倍还是4倍），考研中更常用 "three times as large as" 或 "three times the size of" 这两种无歧义表达。
>
> ---
>
> ### 三、the more...the more（倍增比较）
>
> **结构：** `The + 比较级 + (S + V), the + 比较级 + (S + V)`
>
> | 例句 | 分析 |
> |------|------|
> | **The harder** you work, **the luckier** you get. | 越努力，越幸运 |
> | **The more** data we collect, **the more** accurate our model becomes. | 收集的数据越多，模型越精确 |
> | **The sooner**, **the better**. | 越快越好（极度省略） |
>
> **注意：** 这里的 the 不是冠词，而是副词（= by that much），表示"程度随另一个程度同步增长"。
>
> ---
>
> ### 四、no more...than / no less...than（考研难点）
>
> 这是中国学生最容易混淆的比较结构：
>
> | 结构 | 含义 | 逻辑 | 例句 |
> |------|------|------|------|
> | **no more...than** | A 不是…正如 B 也不是 | **两者都不是** | He is **no more** a scholar **than** I am. = 他不是学者，我也不是。 |
> | **no less...than** | A 确实是…正如 B 也是 | **两者都是** | She is **no less** talented **than** her teacher. = 她和老师一样有才。 |
> | **not more...than** | A 不比 B 更… | 程度比较（A ≤ B） | He is **not more** diligent **than** his brother. = 他不比哥哥更勤奋。 |
> | **not less...than** | A 不比 B 少… | 程度比较（A ≥ B） | He is **not less** diligent **than** his brother. = 他至少和哥哥一样勤奋。 |
>
> **区分口诀：**
> - **no more...than** = 否定双方 → "和…一样不是"
> - **not more...than** = 不超过 → "不比…更"
>
> **理解方式：** no more...than 的 no 修饰的是 more（差距为零），暗示"你以为有差距？没有——两边都不是"。
>
> ---
>
> ### 五、比较从句中的省略（考研阅读核心）
>
> 英语比较中省略重复部分，还原省略是理解长难句的关键：
>
> | 省略后 | 还原 |
> |--------|------|
> | She works harder than **he**. | ...than he **works hard**. |
> | The result was better than **expected**. | ...than **it was** expected **to be**. |
> | More people applied than **were needed**. | 申请人数比需要的多 |
> | He is less interested in theory than **in practice**. | 他对理论的兴趣不如对实践的兴趣 |
>
> **还原步骤：**
> 1. 找到 than/as
> 2. 确定比较的是什么（主语？宾语？程度？）
> 3. 把主句对应部分复制到 than/as 后面，看哪些被省略了
>
> ---
>
> ### 六、rather than / prefer / would rather
>
> | 结构 | 用法 | 例句 |
> |------|------|------|
> | **rather than** + do | 而不是（平行结构） | He chose to stay **rather than** leave. |
> | **prefer** A **to** B | 偏好 A 超过 B | I prefer tea **to** coffee. |
> | **prefer to** do **rather than** do | 宁愿…而不 | I prefer to walk **rather than** drive. |
> | **would rather** do **than** do | 宁愿…而不 | I **would rather** fail **than** cheat. |
>
> **注意 rather than 的词性判断：**
> - 连接两个动词时，后面用原形（平行）
> - 放在句首时，后面的主句才是真正想做的：Rather than wait, she left. = 她没等，走了。

## 实例分析

### 例句 1（考研阅读 — no more...than 双重否定）

> A computer **no more** understands language **than** a calculator understands mathematics — both merely manipulate symbols according to rules.
> — *改编自考研英语一阅读*

**拆解：**
```
A computer [no more] understands language [than] a calculator understands mathematics
              └── no more...than = 两者都不：
                  电脑不理解语言，正如计算器不理解数学。

— both merely manipulate symbols according to rules.
  └── 解释：两者都只是按规则操作符号而已。
```

**中文对照：** 电脑对语言的"理解"，和计算器对数学的"理解"一样——都不是真正的理解，只是按规则操作符号。

---

### 例句 2（考研翻译 — 倍数比较 + 省略还原）

> The urban population of developing countries is now **nearly four times as large as** it was in 1970, a rate of growth **far exceeding** that of developed nations during any comparable period.
> — *改编自考研翻译*

**拆解：**
```
The urban population... is now [nearly four times as large as] it was [large] in 1970,
                                   倍数比较：现在是1970年的近四倍
                                   省略还原：as it was [large]

a rate of growth [far exceeding] that of developed nations...
                   └── 现在分词后置定语修饰 rate
                       that = the rate of growth（代词避免重复）
```

**中文对照：** 发展中国家的城市人口现在是1970年的近四倍，增长速度远超发达国家在任何可比时期的增长速度。

---

### 例句 3（考研阅读 — the more...the more + 复杂省略）

> **The more deeply** a society relies on algorithmic decision-making, **the greater** the risk that human judgment — once considered irreplaceable — will atrophy from disuse.
> — *改编自 The Guardian*

**拆解：**
```
[The more deeply] a society relies on algorithmic decision-making,
  the + 比较级      S              V

[the greater] the risk [that human judgment... will atrophy from disuse]
  the + 比较级    S      └── 同位语从句解释 risk 的内容
                         (is) ← 省略的系动词

插入成分：once considered irreplaceable = 曾被认为不可替代的（过去分词插入语）
```

**中文对照：** 一个社会越深入地依赖算法决策，人类判断力——曾被视为不可替代——因废弃不用而退化的风险就越大。

---

### 例句 4（考研完形 — rather than + 含蓄比较）

> The government opted to subsidize renewable energy **rather than** impose stricter regulations on fossil fuels, a strategy **less likely to provoke** industry backlash **than** outright bans would have been.
> — *改编自 The Economist*

**拆解：**
```
The government opted to subsidize... [rather than] impose...
                                      └── 而不是（两个动词原形平行）

a strategy [less likely to provoke industry backlash]
            └── 形容词短语做后置定语修饰 strategy
               [than outright bans would have been (likely to provoke)]
                └── than 从句，省略了 likely to provoke
```

**中文对照：** 政府选择补贴可再生能源，而非对化石燃料实施更严格的监管——这一策略比直接禁令更不容易引发行业反弹。

## 自检

> [!example] 解析以下句子中的比较结构，还原所有省略，并翻译成中文：
> The research suggests that children exposed to bilingual environments develop cognitive flexibility no less remarkable than that observed in professional musicians, and perhaps more so than was previously assumed — the more diverse the linguistic input, the more resilient the neural pathways become.

> [!success]- 参考答案
>
> **三处比较结构：**
>
> | # | 结构 | 还原省略 |
> |---|------|---------|
> | ① | **no less remarkable than** that observed in... | no less remarkable than the cognitive flexibility [that is] observed in professional musicians [is remarkable]。no less...than = 两者都是 → 不亚于音乐家 |
> | ② | **more so than was previously assumed** | more remarkable than it was previously assumed [to be remarkable]。so = remarkable 的替代词 |
> | ③ | **the more diverse...the more resilient** | The more diverse the linguistic input [is], the more resilient the neural pathways become. 省略了第一个从句的系动词 is |
>
> **中文翻译：**
> 研究表明，在双语环境中成长的儿童发展出的认知灵活性不亚于在专业音乐家身上观察到的水平，甚至可能比先前所设想的更强——语言输入越多样化，神经通路就越具有韧性。
>
> **关键辨析：**
> - that = the cognitive flexibility（代词，避免重复名词）
> - observed = which is observed（缩减的关系从句）
> - so = remarkable（替代词，避免重复形容词）
> - was previously assumed 的主语是 it（形式主语，= that children develop... 这件事）
