# 非谓语动词 (Non-finite Verbs)

## 直觉入口

> [!question] 观察这些句子——每句中哪些动词形式没有充当"谓语"？它们在做什么工作？
>
> **A.** **Driven by curiosity**, the scientist opened the sealed chamber.
>
> **B.** The report **published last week** contains several errors.
>
> **C.** She worked overtime **to meet the deadline**.
>
> **D.** **His hands trembling**, the boy reached for the door.
>
> **E.** **Having exhausted all other options**, the government agreed to negotiate.

试着回答：
1. 每句中加粗的部分包含动词形式，但它们有"时态"吗？有主语吗？
2. 这些部分在主句中充当什么角色？（原因？时间？修饰名词？）
3. 如何判断 driven 的动作是谁发出的？D 句为什么有 his hands？

> [!note]- 我的假设
> *(试着写下：这些非谓语形式和主句之间的"逻辑关系"是什么？哪些有自己的主语，哪些没有？)*

## 中文桥梁

**中文怎么做：**

中文不区分谓语/非谓语——动词可以自由串联，靠语序和语境判断关系：

| 英语（需要标记非谓语形式） | 中文（直接并列动词） |
|---------------------------|-------------------|
| **Driven by** curiosity, he opened the door. | 他**出于**好奇，打开了门。 |
| The report **published** last week... | **上周发表的**报告… |
| She worked hard **to pass** the exam. | 她努力学习**为了**通过考试。 |
| **His hands trembling**, he spoke. | 他**手在抖**，开口说话。 |

**关键差异：**
- 中文：动词并列即可，不需要变形。"出于好奇"、"上周发表的"——动词没有形态变化。
- 英语：一个简单句只能有**一个谓语动词**（带时态的），其余动词必须变成 -ing / -ed / to do 三种"非谓语"形式，标明自己不是主动词。

**为什么英语需要这个机制：**

英语是形态语言——每个动词必须"表态"：你到底是不是这句话的核心谓语？如果不是，就必须用形态变化（-ing/-ed/to do）来"降级"，告诉读者"我不是主干，我只是修饰/补充"。中文没有这个规则，所以中国学生最容易写出"两个谓语打架"的错误句。

## 规则揭示

> [!tip]- 展开核心规则
>
> ### 一、三种非谓语形式及其功能
>
> | 形式 | 长什么样 | 能当什么用 | 核心语义 |
> |------|---------|-----------|---------|
> | **现在分词** | V-ing | 形容词 / 状语 | 主动、进行 |
> | **过去分词** | V-ed (或不规则) | 形容词 / 状语 | 被动、完成 |
> | **不定式** | to + V | 名词 / 形容词 / 状语 | 目的、将来、未实现 |
> | **动名词** | V-ing | 名词 | 动作本身（抽象概念） |
>
> > 现在分词和动名词形式相同（都是 -ing），但功能不同：分词做修饰，动名词做名词。
>
> ---
>
> ### 二、非谓语做状语（考研出现频率最高）
>
> 这是考研长难句中你最常遇到的模式——逗号后/前的 -ing/-ed 短语：
>
> | 类型 | 结构 | 语义 | 例句 |
> |------|------|------|------|
> | -ing 做状语 | `V-ing..., 主句` | 主动（原因/伴随/时间） | **Walking** along the river, I saw a heron. |
> | -ed 做状语 | `V-ed..., 主句` | 被动（原因/条件） | **Frustrated** by the delay, she left. |
> | to do 做状语 | `To V..., 主句` | 目的 / 结果 | **To survive**, animals adapt. |
> | having done | `Having V-ed..., 主句` | 先于主句发生的动作 | **Having finished** the work, he went home. |
>
> **判断语义的方法：** 把非谓语短语还原成完整从句——
> - Walking along the river → **While I was walking** along the river（时间）
> - Frustrated by the delay → **Because she was frustrated** by the delay（原因）
> - To survive → **In order to survive**（目的）
>
> ---
>
> ### 三、逻辑主语规则（核心！考研必考）
>
> **默认规则：** 非谓语动词的逻辑主语 = 主句的主语。
>
> ✅ **Driven** by curiosity, **the scientist** opened the chamber.
> → the scientist 被 driven（被好奇心驱动的人 = 打开房间的人 ✓）
>
> ❌ **Walking** down the street, **the trees** were beautiful.
> → trees 在 walking？逻辑不通 = 悬垂分词错误 (dangling participle)
>
> **修正方法：**
> - Walking down the street, **I** found the trees beautiful. ✓
> - **As I walked** down the street, the trees were beautiful. ✓（还原为从句）
>
> ---
>
> ### 四、独立主格结构 (Absolute Construction)
>
> 当非谓语的逻辑主语 ≠ 主句主语时，必须在非谓语前加上自己的主语：
>
> **公式：** `名词/代词 + V-ing / V-ed / adj / 介词短语, 主句`
>
> | 例句 | 逻辑主语 | 主句主语 |
> |------|---------|---------|
> | **His hands trembling**, he opened the door. | his hands | he |
> | **The work done**, they went home. | the work | they |
> | **Weather permitting**, we'll have a picnic. | weather | we |
> | **All things considered**, the plan is sound. | all things | the plan |
>
> **识别要点：** 逗号前有"名词 + 非谓语形式"，且这个名词不是主句主语 → 独立主格。
>
> ---
>
> ### 五、非谓语做后置定语（= 缩减的关系从句）
>
> | 完整关系从句 | 缩减为非谓语 | 类型 |
> |-------------|-------------|------|
> | the man **who is standing** there | the man **standing** there | -ing（主动） |
> | the report **which was published** last week | the report **published** last week | -ed（被动） |
> | the first person **who arrived** | the first person **to arrive** | to do（序数词触发） |
>
> 后置分词本质上就是关系从句的"压缩版"——去掉关系代词和 be 动词。详见 [[从句缩减]]。
>
> ---
>
> ### 六、分词链 / 并列分词
>
> 多个分词可以并列修饰同一个主句主语：
>
> ```
> Driven by ambition, guided by mentors, constrained by resources,
> she built a company from nothing.
> ```
>
> 三个 -ed 分词短语共享同一个逻辑主语 she。读长难句时看到连续的逗号分隔分词短语，找到主句主语即可。
>
> ---
>
> ### 七、常见非谓语固定表达（考研完形高频）
>
> | 表达 | 含义 |
> |------|------|
> | generally/strictly/broadly **speaking** | 一般/严格/广泛地说 |
> | **given** that... | 考虑到… |
> | **provided/providing** (that)... | 如果/只要… |
> | **compared with/to** | 与…相比 |
> | **judging from/by** | 从…来判断 |
> | **considering** | 考虑到 |

## 实例分析

### 例句 1（考研阅读风格 — 分词做状语 + 逻辑主语）

> **Having been raised in a culture that stresses group harmony**, the Japanese are accustomed to reaching consensus through lengthy discussion.
> — *考研英语一阅读理解*

**拆解：**
```
[Having been raised in a culture [that stresses group harmony]],
  └── 分词状语（having been raised = 先发生 + 被动）
       逻辑主语 = 主句主语 the Japanese
            └── 关系从句修饰 culture

the Japanese  are accustomed to  reaching consensus...
    S              V(系动词)          介词宾语（动名词短语）
```

**中文对照：** 由于在强调群体和谐的文化中被抚养长大，日本人习惯于通过长时间讨论达成共识。

**注意：** having been raised = 完成时被动分词，表示"在主句动作之前就已经被培养"。

---

### 例句 2（学术论文风格 — 独立主格 + 后置分词）

> The experiment was repeated, **each variable controlled independently**, using equipment **donated by the National Science Foundation**.
> — *改编自学术论文*

**拆解：**
```
The experiment was repeated,
    S               V(被动)

[each variable controlled independently],   ← 独立主格（each variable ≠ the experiment）
 名词        过去分词

using equipment [donated by the NSF]         ← -ing 状语（伴随）+ 后置分词修饰 equipment
                  └── 缩减的关系从句 = which was donated by...
```

**中文对照：** 实验被重复进行，每个变量被独立控制，使用的是国家科学基金会捐赠的设备。

---

### 例句 3（考研翻译风格 — 多重非谓语嵌套）

> **Concerned that rising costs would erode profits**, the board voted **to restructure the company**, **eliminating** redundant positions while **preserving** core research teams.
> — *改编自 The Economist*

**拆解：**
```
[Concerned that rising costs would erode profits],  ← -ed 分词状语（原因），逻辑主语 = the board
                    └── that 从句作 concerned 的补语

the board  voted  [to restructure the company],     ← 不定式做宾语
    S        V

[eliminating redundant positions]                   ← -ing 分词状语（结果/伴随）
[while preserving core research teams]              ← while + -ing（让步/对比）
```

**中文对照：** 由于担心成本上升会侵蚀利润，董事会投票决定重组公司，在保留核心研究团队的同时裁撤冗余岗位。

---

### 例句 4（分词链 — 用逗号分隔的典型考研长难句模式）

> **Shaped by decades of research**, **informed by real-world data**, and **validated through rigorous testing**, the new framework offers a more reliable approach to climate prediction.
> — *改编自 Nature*

**拆解：**
```
[Shaped by decades of research],       ← -ed 分词（被动）
[informed by real-world data],          ← -ed 分词（被动）
and [validated through rigorous testing], ← -ed 分词（被动）
                                         三个并列分词共享逻辑主语 the new framework

the new framework  offers  a more reliable approach...
       S              V           O
```

**这就是你之前问的"逗号分隔模式"的典型形态。** 识别要点：逗号前的片段没有独立主谓 → 它们是分词短语，逻辑主语就是后面主句的主语。

## 自检

> [!example] 解析这个句子中所有的非谓语动词，标出每个的逻辑主语和语法功能：
> Faced with mounting evidence suggesting that the policy had failed, the committee, its confidence shaken, voted to abandon the initiative, hoping to redirect funds toward programs designed to address the underlying causes.

> [!success]- 参考答案
>
> **共 6 个非谓语动词：**
>
> ```
> [Faced with mounting evidence...]   ← ① -ed 分词状语（原因），逻辑主语 = the committee
>   [suggesting that the policy had failed]  ← ② -ing 后置定语，修饰 evidence（= which suggested）
>
> the committee, [its confidence shaken], voted  ← ③ 独立主格（its confidence ≠ the committee 本身）
>
>   [to abandon the initiative],   ← ④ 不定式，voted 的宾语
>
>   [hoping to redirect funds...]  ← ⑤ -ing 分词状语（伴随/目的），逻辑主语 = the committee
>
>     toward programs [designed to address the underlying causes]  ← ⑥ -ed 后置定语，修饰 programs（= which were designed to...）
> ```
>
> | # | 形式 | 功能 | 逻辑主语 |
> |---|------|------|---------|
> | ① | Faced (V-ed) | 原因状语 | the committee |
> | ② | suggesting (V-ing) | 后置定语→evidence | evidence |
> | ③ | shaken (V-ed) | 独立主格→confidence | its confidence |
> | ④ | to abandon (to V) | 宾语 | the committee |
> | ⑤ | hoping (V-ing) | 伴随状语 | the committee |
> | ⑥ | designed (V-ed) | 后置定语→programs | programs |
