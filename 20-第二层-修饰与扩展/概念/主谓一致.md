# 主谓一致 (Subject-Verb Agreement)

## 直觉入口

> [!question] 以下句子中动词该用单数还是复数？为什么？
>
> **A.** The number of students _ (has/have) increased.
>
> **B.** A number of students _ (has/have) complained.
>
> **C.** Neither the teacher nor the students _ (was/were) informed.
>
> **D.** What matters most _ (is/are) your actions.
>
> **E.** Economics _ (is/are) a challenging subject.

试着回答：
1. A 和 B 长得几乎一样——为什么答案不同？
2. C 中 neither...nor 连接了单数和复数——动词跟谁一致？
3. E 中 economics 以 -s 结尾——是复数吗？

> [!note]- 我的假设
> *(主谓一致到底是跟"意义上的主语"一致，还是跟"语法形式上的主语"一致？)*

---

## 中文桥梁

**中文怎么做：**

中文动词不区分单复数——完全不存在"主谓一致"的概念：

| 英语（动词必须呼应主语） | 中文（动词不变） |
|------------------------|----------------|
| The student **is** ready. / The students **are** ready. | 学生准备好了。（一个或多个都一样） |
| He **goes**. / They **go**. | 他去。/ 他们去。（"去"不变） |

**为什么中国学生在这里出错：**

不是因为规则难，而是因为中文大脑根本没有"检查主语单复数 → 调整动词"这条回路。在写作中容易无意识忽略；在完形填空中成为区分选项的"隐形考点"。

---

## 规则揭示

> [!tip]- 展开核心规则
>
> ### 一、基本原则
>
> **动词与主语的"头名词"(head noun) 一致，不与中间插入的修饰语一致。**
>
> | 例句 | 主语头名词 | 动词 |
> |------|-----------|------|
> | The cost **of the materials** has risen. | cost（单数） | has |
> | The students **in this class** are diligent. | students（复数） | are |
> | A series **of experiments** was conducted. | series（单数） | was |
>
> **陷阱：** 介词短语中的名词不是主语！"of the materials" 是修饰语，真正主语是 cost。
>
> ---
>
> ### 二、高频考点分类
>
> #### The number of vs A number of
>
> | 结构 | 主语 | 含义 | 动词 |
> |------|------|------|------|
> | **The number** of students | the number | 数量（单数概念） | 单数 |
> | **A number** of students | students | 许多学生（复数概念） | 复数 |
>
> #### 不定代词
>
> | 永远单数 | 永远复数 | 看情况 |
> |---------|---------|--------|
> | everyone, everybody, someone, anyone, nobody, each, every, either, neither | both, few, many, several | all, some, none, most, any（看 of 后面的名词） |
>
> - **Everyone** has a phone.（单数）
> - **Both** of them are here.（复数）
> - **Some** of the water **is** contaminated.（不可数 → 单数）
> - **Some** of the students **are** absent.（可数复数 → 复数）
>
> #### 就近原则 (Proximity)
>
> | 结构 | 规则 | 例句 |
> |------|------|------|
> | either A **or** B | 动词与 B 一致 | Either you or he **is** responsible. |
> | neither A **nor** B | 动词与 B 一致 | Neither the teacher nor the students **were** aware. |
> | not only A **but also** B | 动词与 B 一致 | Not only the students but also the teacher **was** surprised. |
> | there be + A and B | 动词与 A 一致 | There **is** a book and two pens on the desk. |
>
> ---
>
> ### 三、特殊主语
>
> #### 集体名词（可单可复）
>
> | 名词 | 单数用法（整体） | 复数用法（个体成员） |
> |------|----------------|-------------------|
> | team, family, committee, government, staff | The team **is** well-prepared.（作为整体） | The team **are** arguing among themselves.（英式，强调成员） |
>
> 考研中默认用单数（美式用法）。
>
> #### -s 结尾但单数的词
>
> | 单词 | 含义 | 例句 |
> |------|------|------|
> | economics, politics, physics, mathematics | 学科名 | Economics **is** fascinating. |
> | news | 新闻 | The news **is** shocking. |
> | the United States | 国名 | The United States **has** announced... |
>
> #### 名词从句做主语 → 单数
>
> | 例句 | 分析 |
> |------|------|
> | **What we need** is more data. | what 从句做主语 → 单数 |
> | **That he lied** is obvious. | that 从句做主语 → 单数 |
> | **Whether she agrees** remains to be seen. | whether 从句 → 单数 |
>
> #### 带 and 的特殊情况
>
> | 情况 | 动词 | 例句 |
> |------|------|------|
> | A and B = 两个不同事物 | 复数 | Tom **and** Jerry **are** friends. |
> | A and B = 同一事物/概念 | 单数 | Bread and butter **is** my breakfast. |
> | every/each + A and B | 单数 | **Every** boy and girl **has** a book. |
>
> ---
>
> ### 四、考研完形中的隐藏考法
>
> 考研不会直接问"该用 is 还是 are"——它通常这样考：
>
> 1. **长距离一致**：主语和动词之间插入很长的修饰语，让你忘记主语是什么
>    > The **impact** of rising sea levels on coastal communities in Southeast Asia **has** been...
>
> 2. **倒装中一致**：倒装后主语在动词后面
>    > Among the factors that contributed to the crisis **were** poor regulation and excessive risk-taking.
>    > （主语是 poor regulation and excessive risk-taking → 复数）
>
> 3. **there be 结构**：动词与后面第一个名词一致
>    > There **remains** considerable debate about the issue.（debate 单数）

## 实例分析

### 例句 1（长距离一致 — 考研完形典型陷阱）

> The **effectiveness** of these policies in addressing the complex and deeply rooted social inequalities that have persisted across generations **remains** a subject of intense debate among scholars.
> — *改编自考研完形*

**分析：**
```
主语：The effectiveness（单数）
干扰：of these policies / in addressing... / that have persisted... 
  → 中间插了20多个词，但动词必须与 effectiveness 一致 → remains（单数）
```

---

### 例句 2（倒装中的主谓一致）

> **Underlying** this transformation **are** a series of technological innovations and a fundamental shift in consumer expectations.
> — *改编自 The Economist*

**分析：**
```
正常语序：A series of innovations and a fundamental shift are underlying this transformation.
倒装后：Underlying this transformation are [主语].
主语 = A series... and a shift（两个名词并列 → 复数 → are）
```

---

### 例句 3（不定代词 + of 结构）

> **None** of the evidence **suggests** that the treatment is harmful, **nor** **does** any of the data support the claim that side effects are widespread.
> — *改编自学术论文*

**分析：**
```
None of the evidence → evidence 不可数 → suggests（单数）
nor does any of the data → data（学术中常作不可数/单数）→ does（单数）
注意：nor 引起倒装（does 提前）
```

## 自检

> [!example] 选择正确的动词形式并解释判断依据：
> 1. The majority of the population (is/are) opposed to the new law.
> 2. A range of options (has/have) been proposed, but neither the committee nor the advisory boards (has/have) reached a consensus.
> 3. What (concerns/concern) many economists (is/are) not the current data but the long-term trends that (suggests/suggest) a structural shift (is/are) underway.

> [!success]- 参考答案
>
> **1.** The majority of the population **is** opposed...
> - population 不可数/集体 → 单数。（若为 the majority of the voters → are，因为 voters 可数复数）
>
> **2.** A range of options **has** been proposed, but neither the committee nor the advisory boards **have** reached a consensus.
> - a range = 主语头名词（单数）→ has
> - neither A nor B → 与 B 一致 → boards（复数）→ have
>
> **3.** What **concerns** many economists **is** not the current data but the long-term trends that **suggest** a structural shift **is** underway.
> - What concerns → what 从句做主语（单数）→ concerns（从句内部动词与 what 一致）
> - What... is → 整个 what 从句做主句主语 → is（单数）
> - trends that suggest → trends 复数 → suggest
> - a structural shift is → shift 单数 → is
