## 非谓语动词

### 真题 1（2010 翻译）

> **Lacking** a magnetic-Loss environment, research scientists **exploring** a new source of energy for satellites are **forced to** rely on batteries **designed to generate** electricity from sunlight.

**语法拆解：**
- **Lacking** a magnetic... → -ing 分词状语（原因），逻辑主语 = research scientists
- scientists **exploring** a new source → -ing 后置定语（= who are exploring）
- **forced to** rely on → 被动（are forced），含不定式（to rely）
- batteries **designed to generate** → -ed 后置定语（= which are designed to generate）

**涉及：** [[非谓语动词]] [[从句缩减]]

---

### 真题 2（2015 阅读）

> **Having been raised in a culture that stresses group harmony**, the Japanese are accustomed to **reaching** consensus through lengthy discussion, **seeking** common ground rather than **defending** individual positions.

**语法拆解：**
- **Having been raised** → having done 被动分词（先于主句的被动经历）
- a culture **that stresses**... → 关系从句修饰 culture
- accustomed to **reaching** → 动名词做介词 to 的宾语
- **seeking**...**rather than defending** → 并列分词状语（伴随）+ rather than 连接平行结构

**涉及：** [[非谓语动词]] [[关系从句]] [[并列与从属]]

## 虚拟语气

### 真题 3（2013 翻译）

> **Had it not been for** the timely investment from the government, our company **would not have survived** the financial crisis.

**语法拆解：**
- **Had it not been for** = If it had not been for（倒装虚拟，与过去相反）
- **would not have survived** = 虚拟主句（过去虚拟结果）

**涉及：** [[虚拟语气]] [[倒装]]

---

### 真题 4（2016 完形）

> The board recommended that the company **allocate** more resources to research and development, a suggestion that **was** initially met with resistance.

**语法拆解：**
- recommended that...allocate → should 型虚拟（allocate 用原形）
- a suggestion **that was met with** → 关系从句修饰 suggestion（that = 主语）

**涉及：** [[虚拟语气]] [[名词从句]] [[关系从句]]

## 比较结构

### 真题 5（2014 阅读）

> The correlation between education levels and income is **no more** a proof of causation **than** the correlation between ice cream sales and drowning deaths.

**语法拆解：**
- **no more A than B** = A 不是，正如 B 也不是 → 两者都不能证明因果
- 真实含义：教育水平与收入的相关性不能证明因果关系，正如冰淇淋销量与溺水死亡的相关性一样。

**涉及：** [[比较结构]]

## 从句嵌套

### 真题 6（2018 阅读）

> **What** the researchers **found** was **that** children **who** were exposed to music training **showed** improvements in areas **that** had **nothing** to do with music — specifically, in the executive functions **that** govern planning, organization, and the ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously.

**语法拆解：**
- **What** the researchers found → what 名词从句（做主句主语）
- was **that** children... → that 表语从句
- children **who** were exposed to... → 关系从句修饰 children
- areas **that** had nothing to do with → 关系从句修饰 areas
- executive functions **that** govern... → 关系从句修饰 functions
- the ability **to manage** → 不定式做后置定语

**涉及：** [[名词从句]] [[关系从句]] [[非谓语动词]]

## 省略与特殊结构

### 真题 7（2019 翻译）

> **It was not until** the early twentieth century **that** scientists began to understand **how** the brain processes language — and even today, **far more remains unknown than understood**.

**语法拆解：**
- **It was not until...that** → 强调句（强调时间 not until）
- **how** the brain processes → 名词从句（understand 的宾语）
- far more remains unknown **than understood** → 比较省略（= than [what] is understood）

**涉及：** [[分裂句]] [[名词从句]] [[比较结构]] [[省略与共享]]

## 并列与信息权重

### 真题 8（2020 阅读）

> **Although** proponents of artificial intelligence point to its potential to eliminate bias in hiring decisions, critics argue that algorithms merely **automate** existing prejudices rather than **correct** them — and that **without** deliberate intervention, such systems may **entrench** inequality rather than **reduce** it.

**语法拆解：**
- **Although** A, B → 让步从属（A = 已知观点，B = 作者倾向的观点）
- critics argue **that** X **and that** Y → 两个并列的宾语从句
- **automate** rather than **correct** / **entrench** rather than **reduce** → rather than 平行结构
- **without** deliberate intervention → 含蓄虚拟条件

**涉及：** [[并列与从属]] [[名词从句]] [[虚拟语气]]
