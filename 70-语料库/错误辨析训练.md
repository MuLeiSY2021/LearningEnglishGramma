# 错误辨析训练

> 这是一种新的练习类型：找出句子中的**语法错误**，诊断错误原因，并给出改正。
> 考研完形填空和改错题的核心技能就是这个——快速识别"哪里不对"。

---

## Level 1: 单一错误（只有一个问题）

找出错误，说明错误类型，给出改正。

> **1.** "The informations provided by the witness was crucial to the investigation."

> **2.** "Despite of the rain, the match continued as scheduled."

> **3.** "He suggested that she takes a different approach to the problem."

> **4.** "The students, along with their teacher, has arrived at the museum."

> **5.** "Having not completed the assignment, the professor gave him a failing grade."

> **6.** "The book who I borrowed from the library is overdue."

> **7.** "She is one of the few scientists who has won the award twice."

> **8.** "Not until he arrived at the airport that he realized his passport was missing."

---

## Level 2: 双重错误（每句有两处问题）

> **9.** "The amount of students who is enrolled in the program have increased this year."

> **10.** "If he would have studied harder, he might pass the exam."

---

## Level 3: 判断对错（有些句子是对的！）

标出哪些是正确的，哪些有错误。

> **11.** "The committee have decided to postpone the meeting." 

> **12.** "He insisted that the report be submitted by Friday."

> **13.** "The data suggests a strong correlation between the two variables."

> **14.** "Laying on the couch, she watched TV all afternoon."

---

## 任务

> [!question] 你的任务：
> 1. 找出每个错误的**位置**
> 2. 说明**错误类型**（主谓一致/非谓语/时态/冠词/介词/从句/虚拟语气...）
> 3. 给出**改正**和改正理由
> 4. Level 3 中标出"正确"的句子并说明为什么它看起来可疑但其实没错

## 提示

> [!hint]- 提示 1（轻）
> Level 1 的错误类型分布：
> - 1: 名词可数性
> - 2: 介词搭配
> - 3: 虚拟语气
> - 4: 主谓一致
> - 5: 非谓语逻辑主语（悬垂修饰语）
> - 6: 关系代词选择
> - 7: 主谓一致（陷阱！）
> - 8: 倒装结构

> [!hint]- 提示 2（中）
> 句 5 的悬垂修饰语问题：
> - "Having not completed..." 的逻辑主语应该是谁？→ 学生（他）
> - 但主句主语是 "the professor"（教授）
> - 教授没有"未完成作业"——学生才是
>
> 句 7 的陷阱：
> - "one of the few scientists who..." → who 修饰的是 scientists（复数）还是 one（单数）？
> - 正确：who 修饰 scientists → 动词用 **have**（复数）
> - 逻辑：赢过两次奖的是那些科学家们（她是其中之一），不是"一个"

> [!hint]- 提示 3（重）
> Level 3 中正确的句子：
> - 11: ✓ 在英式英语中 committee 可以用复数动词（集体名词指成员）
> - 12: ✓ insisted that + 原形动词 = should 型虚拟语气（be 是正确的）
> - 13: 争议句——data 传统上是复数（datum 的复数），但现代英语中普遍接受 data + 单数动词。考研中两种都可能出现。

## 解答

> [!success]- 完整解析
>
> ## Level 1
>
> | # | 错误位置 | 错误类型 | 改正 | 解释 |
> |---|---------|---------|------|------|
> | 1 | informations | 名词可数性 | **information**（不可数） | information 永远不加 s，不可数名词 |
> | 2 | Despite of | 介词搭配 | **Despite** the rain 或 **In spite of** the rain | despite 后直接跟名词，不加 of；in spite of 才需要 of |
> | 3 | takes | 虚拟语气 | **take**（原形） | suggest + that + 原形（should 型虚拟） |
> | 4 | has | 主谓一致 | **have** arrived | "along with" 不改变主语的数——主语是 students（复数）|
> | 5 | 逻辑主语错位 | 悬垂修饰语 | **Having not completed the assignment, he received a failing grade from the professor.** | 分词短语的逻辑主语必须=主句主语。另外 "having not" 也应改为 "not having" |
> | 6 | who | 关系代词 | **that/which** | 修饰物（book）不能用 who，who 只修饰人 |
> | 7 | has | 主谓一致 | **have** won | "scientists who have won"——who 指代 scientists（复数）|
> | 8 | that he realized | 倒装结构 | **did he realize** | Not until... + 倒装（did + S + V），不是 that 引导 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 详解关键错误
>
> **句 4：along with / together with / as well as 不影响主谓一致**
>
> | 连接词 | 影响主语数？ | 例子 |
> |--------|------------|------|
> | **and** | 是（变复数） | The teacher **and** the students **have** arrived. |
> | **along with** | 否（主语不变） | The students, **along with** their teacher, **have** arrived. |
> | **as well as** | 否 | He, **as well as** his friends, **is** coming. |
> | **together with** | 否 | The manager, **together with** her team, **was** present. |
>
> **句 7：one of the + 复数名词 + who 从句**
>
> > "She is one of the few scientists **who have** won the award twice."
>
> 逻辑：谁赢了两次？→ scientists（不止她一个人赢过两次，她是其中之一）。所以 who = scientists → have。
>
> 但注意：如果是 "She is **the only one** of the scientists who **has** won twice" → has（因为只有她一个人赢过两次）。
>
> **句 8：Not until 的正确句式**
>
> ```
> ❌ Not until he arrived at the airport that he realized...
> ✓  Not until he arrived at the airport did he realize...
>
> Not until + 时间从句 + 倒装主句（did/was/had + S + V）
> ```
>
> 不要和强调句混淆：
> - 强调句：**It was** not until he arrived **that** he realized...（有 It was...that 框架）
> - 倒装句：Not until he arrived **did** he realize...（前置否定 + 倒装）
>
> ---
>
> ## Level 2
>
> ### 句 9（双重错误）
>
> > "The **amount** of students who **is** enrolled..."
>
> | 错误 | 改正 | 原因 |
> |------|------|------|
> | amount → **number** | amount 用于不可数名词，number 用于可数名词 | students 可数 |
> | is → **are** | who 指代 students（复数）| 关系从句中的动词与先行词一致 |
>
> 改正全句："The **number** of students who **are** enrolled in the program **has** increased this year."
>
> 注意最后的 has：the number（主语核心词，单数）→ has increased。
>
> ---
>
> ### 句 10（双重错误）
>
> > "If he **would have** studied harder, he might **pass** the exam."
>
> | 错误 | 改正 | 原因 |
> |------|------|------|
> | would have studied → **had studied** | 虚拟条件句中 if 从句不用 would | 与过去相反：If + had done |
> | might pass → **might have passed** | 主句也必须用过去虚拟 | would/could/might + have done |
>
> 改正全句："If he **had studied** harder, he **might have passed** the exam."
>
> ---
>
> ## Level 3: 判断对错
>
> | # | 判定 | 解释 |
> |---|------|------|
> | 11 | ✓ **正确**（英式） | 英式英语中 committee 可作复数（指成员）。美式偏好 has decided。两种都可接受。 |
> | 12 | ✓ **正确** | insisted that + be（原形）= should 型虚拟。看起来像错误（为什么不用 is/was？），但这正是虚拟语气的标志。 |
> | 13 | ✓ **现代英语可接受** | 传统语法：data = 复数（datum 的复数），应该用 suggest。但现代学术英语中 data + 单数动词已被广泛接受。考研中两种答案都可能出现。 |
> | 14 | ❌ **错误** | **Laying** → **Lying**。lay = 放置（及物，需要宾语），lie = 躺（不及物）。"躺在沙发上"= lying on the couch。 |
>
> ---
>
> ## 错误识别的系统方法
>
> ```
> 读句子时检查清单：
> 1. 主谓一致？ → 找核心主语，判断单复数
> 2. 时态/虚拟？ → if从句不用would，suggest/insist用原形
> 3. 非谓语？ → 逻辑主语=主句主语？lay/lie/raise/rise区分？
> 4. 关系代词？ → 人who/物which/两者that
> 5. 介词搭配？ → despite≠despite of, consist of≠consist in
> 6. 可数/不可数？ → information/advice/furniture/equipment 不可数
> 7. 句式结构？ → 倒装句的语序(did+S+V)
> ```
